  4. Take 10 g salt
  5. Take 120 g caster sugar
  6. Make ready 25 g vegetable oil
  7. Make ready 2 eggs
  8. Prepare 1 packet sesame/poppy seeds

Instructions to make Sweeten Seeded Bread Rolls:
  1. Assemble all the required ingredients
  2. Weigh the ingredients appropiately
  3. Sieve the flour
  4. In a bowl,dissolve the yeast in half the water
  5. In another bowl,dissolve salt and sugar in the other half
  6. Add both liquids to the flour
  7. Carefully knead by hand
  8. When the dough is ready,cover it with a dump cloth
  9. Leave it to prove for 30 minutes
  10. After 30 minutes,scale it into desired portions
  11. Shape and place on a greased baking sheet
  12. Cover them again
  13. Let them prove until almost double in size
  14. Eggwash carefully
  15. Sprinkle on top with either sesame seeds or poppy seeds
  16. Bake immediately at 230 degrees
  17. Break one and open to test if cooked
  18. Allow to cool
  19. Serve as your tea-snack
  20. Enjoy your breakfast
