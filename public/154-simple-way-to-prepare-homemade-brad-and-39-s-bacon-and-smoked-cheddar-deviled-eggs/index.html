  1. Prepare 18 xtra lg eggs
  2. Take 1/2 cup shredded smoked cheddar
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup peppered bacon, cooked and crumbled
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup sour cream
  5. Take 1/4 cup mayonnaise

  7. Prepare 1 tsp granulated garlic

Steps to make Brad's bacon and smoked cheddar deviled eggs:
  1. Hard boil eggs, then peel. Cut in half. Remove yolks to a bowl and plate the egg whites.
  2. Mix yolks with the rest of ingredients.
  3. Fill whites with mixture. Refrigerate for an hour. Serve and enjoy.
