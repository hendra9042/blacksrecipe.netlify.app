The ingredients needed to make Fried ham and egg salad:
  1. Make ready 1/3 lb ground smoked ham
  2. Get 2 large eggs
  3. Get 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  4. Prepare 1 tsp yellow prepared mustard
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp dill weed
  6. Take 1/4 tsp cilantro
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp chopped parsley
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
