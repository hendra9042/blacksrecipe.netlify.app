The ingredients needed to make Scotch eggs:
  1. Make ready 2 eggs
  2. Take 1/2 onion
  3. Take 10 g butter
  4. Prepare For the meat patty
  5. Make ready 200 g minced meat (beef or pork)
  6. Take 1/2 beaten egg
  7. Take 5 tbsp breadcrumbs
  8. Prepare Pinch nutmeg
  9. Prepare Pinch salt and pepper
  10. Make ready For the coating
  11. Take 30 g flour
  12. Make ready 1 beaten egg
  13. Make ready 70 g breadcrumbs
  14. Make ready Oil (to deep fry)

Instructions to make Scotch eggs:
  1. Chop the onion into fine pieces and sautée with butter in a heated pan. Take the onions out of the pan and let them cool.
  2. Boil 2 eggs in a small pot for about 6 mins. Once boiled, dip them in ice water, peel the shells off, and dip the peeled eggs in ice water again.
  3. Mix the sautéed onions and all the ingredients for the meat patty in a bowl.
  4. Take the boiled eggs out of the ice water, wipe them with kitchen paper, lightly coat them with flour, and wrap each with the meat patty. Shape each like a football.
  5. Coat each ball with flour, beaten egg, and breadcrumbs (in the order written) and deep fry until golden brown. Serve with your choice of sauce!
