The ingredients needed to make Avocado toast with poached eggs & bacon:
  1. Get 1/2 ripe avocado
  2. Make ready 2 slices of multi grain bread
  3. Prepare lettuce leaves
  4. Take 4 slice bacon
  5. Prepare 3 slice tomato
  6. Make ready 2 eggs
  7. Take 1/2 tsp vinegar
  8. Get pinch salt & pepper

These take poached eggs to a new level :) Use thick slices of a wholegrain bread or use whatever you have on hand or prefer. Makes a nice meal for brunch, lunch or even an easy dinner :) The cooking time is for bringing the water to a simmer and poaching the eggs. If you are short of time or don't have lemon or lime juice just mash half an avocado straight onto each slice of toast, sprinkle. Sprinkle with sea salt and freshly turned pepper.

Instructions to make Avocado toast with poached eggs & bacon:
  1. Bring water and vinegar to a boil in a small pot.
  2. Heat pan and cook bacon slices.
  3. Slice avocados and tomatoes. Set aside
  4. Toast bread slices.
  5. Gently put eggs into boiling water. Turn off heat immediately.
  6. Assemble ingredients together by placing avocado slices on toast first followed by tomato slices, 2 strips of bacon on each slice of bread, lettuce leaves, and poached eggs. Finish with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  7. ENJOY!
