The ingredients needed to make Sundubu Jjigae (Korean Soft Tofu Stew):
  1. Prepare 800 ml Chicken soup stock
  2. Get 1 tsp Gochujang
  3. Get 1 block Tofu
  4. Take 50 grams Manila clams
  5. Get 1/2 packet Shimeji mushrooms
  6. Get 80 grams Oysters
  7. Take 2 Ocellated octopus
  8. Get 1 Egg yolk
  9. Prepare 1 portion Ramen noodles
  10. Take Dadaegi (Korean chili paste)
  11. Take 1 tbsp Sesame oil
  12. Prepare 1 tbsp Korean chili peppers (fine ground)
  13. Take 1 tsp Soy sauce
  14. Make ready 2 tsp Sugar
  15. Get 1 clove Grated garlic
