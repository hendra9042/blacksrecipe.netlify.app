Steps to make 'Tex max' egg rolls:
  1. Mix all the ingredients( with out the chicken) in a bowl
  2. Cut the chicken breasts into chunks and cook in a pen with spices
  3. Chopped the chunks of the cooked chicken breast and put into the bowl with the rest of the ingredients
  4. Take a spoon from the mixture and put inside one egg roll wrap. Roll the wrap and close it with a little bit of water
  5. Fried in a pan with hot oil until it's golden.
