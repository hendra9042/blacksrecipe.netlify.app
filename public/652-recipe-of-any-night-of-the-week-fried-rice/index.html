The ingredients needed to make Fried Rice:
  1. Take 2 cups cooked rice
  2. Get 1 handful cabbage chopped
  3. Prepare 1 pc ham sliced squares
  4. Take 4 cloves garlic chopped
  5. Prepare 1/2 carrot chopped
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  7. Take 2 pcs chikuwa(japanese rolled fish cake) sliced
  8. Take 1 egg beaten
  9. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp cooking oil
