The ingredients needed to make Fried rice:
  1. Prepare 2 c uncooked rice
  2. Make ready 2 c chicken stock
  3. Prepare 1-1/2 c water
  4. Prepare 1 c frozen corn
  5. Take 1 c frozen peas
  6. Take 2-3 carrots, chopped
  7. Make ready 1/2 onion, chopped
  8. Prepare 4 mushrooms, diced
  9. Prepare 5-6 eggs
  10. Prepare 1 x 8 oz can water chestnuts
  11. Prepare 1/4 c raisins, or to taste
  12. Make ready 1/4 c slivered almonds or cashews
  13. Make ready 1/4-1/2 c soy sauce
  14. Get 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp sesame oil
  16. Prepare 1/2-1 tsp ginger powder

Steps to make Fried rice:
  1. Add rice, chicken stock and water to a rice cooker and cook until done. For best results, cook rice 1 day ahead of making dish and refrigerate over night. If you can’t refrigerate over night, refrigerate after cooking for at least a couple hours until rice feels cold.
  2. Cook corn, peas, carrots, onions, and mushrooms in skillet over medium heat until cooked through.
  3. Whisk eggs in a small bowl. Move veggies to side of pan and add egg to other side of pan. Heat until cooked through.
  4. Add all other ingredients and stir until combined. Cook until heated through.
