The ingredients needed to make Egg Spring Rolls for Cherry Blossom Viewing Bentos:
  1. Take 5 Eggs
  2. Take 8 Spring roll wrappers
  3. Make ready 3 tbsp Mayonnaise
  4. Get 1 tsp Sugar
  5. Prepare 1 sprinkle Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Egg Spring Rolls for Cherry Blossom Viewing Bentos:
  1. First, make some hard-boiled eggs. Make them nice and hard.
  2. Once boiled, peel off the shell and mash. I use a fork for this.
  3. Mix in the mayonnaise and sugar, and add salt and pepper to taste. I think quite a strong flavour is best.
  4. Wrap the egg mixture in the spring roll wrappers and deep fry! They're ready!
