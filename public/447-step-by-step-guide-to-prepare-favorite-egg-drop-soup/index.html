The ingredients needed to make Egg Drop Soup:
  1. Make ready 3 cup chicken broth
  2. Prepare 1 cup water
  3. Take 2 each eggs
  4. Make ready 1 salt
  5. Take 1 white pepper
  6. Get 1 dash ground ginger
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup water
  9. Prepare 1 each green onion, chopped

Instructions to make Egg Drop Soup:
  1. In a medium saucepan bring chicken broth and water to a boil.
  2. Stir in eggs slowly in a circular motion
  3. Mix cornstarch and 1/4 cup of water and add to soup
  4. Stir to desired consistency and season to taste
  5. Transfer to serving bowls, top with green onions, and serve. Can also top with crispy wontons.
