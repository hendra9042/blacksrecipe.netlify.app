The ingredients needed to make Egg Drop soup:
  1. Take 4 cups chicken stock
  2. Make ready 1 egg beaten
  3. Get 1 packet tomato noodles soup mix
  4. Prepare 1 cup chicken cooked shredded
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp vinegar
  6. Take 1 tsp green chill sauce
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp soya sauce
  8. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Get 1/4 cup spring onions finely sliced to garnish

Instructions to make Egg Drop soup:
  1. Put the stock in a saucepan and heat gently, sprinkle the soup mix over it and stir to prevent lumps bring to a boil cook for 5 minutes then stir in the chicken
  2. Add the beaten egg slowly in a thin stream while stirring the hot soup cook for 2 minutes
  3. Season with vinegar, chill sauce,soy sauce,salt and pepper
