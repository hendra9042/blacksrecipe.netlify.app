The ingredients needed to make Easy egg and onion fry masala:
  1. Take 4 green chilles
  2. Make ready 8 eggs boiled and deshelled
  3. Make ready 2 large onions, chopped
  4. Take 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  5. Take Cilantro
  6. Get 1 tbsp garam masala or any kind of masala
  7. Take to taste Salt
  8. Get 1 tbsp chili powder
  9. Prepare oil

The onions have to be golden brown. Add eggs, add salt, red chilli powder, turmeric powder and fry all side until golden brown. Boil the eggs in a pan and keep it aside. In a pan, add oil, when it is heated, add mixed herbs, onion saute it till the onion becomes golden brown.

Instructions to make Easy egg and onion fry masala:
  1. Boil the eggs and peeled d shell and set a side
  2. In a heavy bottom pan, add oil and fry onions and green chilles till brown caramalised
  3. Add ginger garlic paste and fry till raw smells goes off
  4. Add garam masala.,chilli powder and salt according to taste
  5. In another pan, heat oil, fry the boiled eggs for 3 to 4 mins with chilli powder .
  6. Add the eggs to the pan
  7. Cover with a lid and simmer for 3to 5 mins
  8. Serve hot with chapati and rotis or rice
