The ingredients needed to make Special Fried Rice (Chinese):
  1. Prepare Special Fried Rice
  2. Prepare Peanut or EVO Oil
  3. Prepare 1 Ham Steak - Diced (100g)
  4. Prepare 1 Cup Peas, Corn & Carrot (Mixed Veg Frozen)
  5. Prepare 2 cups Chilled cooked Rice - Basmarti or Jasmine
  6. Take 1.5 Tbls Soy Sauce
  7. Prepare 1 Cup Snow Peas (cut into 3/4inch squares)
  8. Get Half Cup Bean Sprouts (string removed)
  9. Get Half Cup Spring Onions (Chopped +save sprinkle for serving deco)
  10. Take 3 Eggs (wisked +salt & pepper)
  11. Prepare Pinch Salt for taste

Instructions to make Special Fried Rice (Chinese):
  1. Add oil & heat frypan/wok on High - Wisk Eggs & add Salt & Pepper for taste. Pour egg into Hot frypan/wok and tilt egg mix until bottom of frypan/wok is covered. Flipping in sides of omlett until egg is fully cooked but not dried out. Remove from pan, slice omlett into strips & set aside.
  2. Add oil & heat frypan/wok on high. Add diced ham stir & fry for a minute, add mixed vegies & sweat down. Add in chilled pre-cooked rice and mix. Add the Soy Sauce, stir thoroughly. Add Snow Peas, Sprouts & heat until cooked through. Add Egg & Shallots in the last minutes, mix through & serve hot.
  3. Cooked Prawns, Chinese Pork or Chicken slices can be added and mixed through the rice in the last minutes of the cook also.
