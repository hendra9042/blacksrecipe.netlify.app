Instructions to make Runny fried  eggs:
  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Crack egg(s) in pan and cook for 3 minutes uncovered (add seasonings — optional — at this time).
  3. Then cover with a lid in the last 1-2 minutes to help the whites cook while the yolk stays soft for a “sunny side up” with cooked whites.
  4. Serve immediately
