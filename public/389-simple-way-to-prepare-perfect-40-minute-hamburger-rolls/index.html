The ingredients needed to make 40 - Minute Hamburger Rolls …..:
  1. Prepare 2 tbsp active dry yeast
  2. Make ready 1 cup plus 2- Tbsp. warm water (110°- 115° )
  3. Get 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  4. Take 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  5. Take 1 egg- beaten
  6. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  7. Take 3 - 1/2 cups all purpose flour

Steps to make 40 - Minute Hamburger Rolls …..:
  1. In large bowl. Dissolve yeast in warm water. Add oil, sugar. Let stand 5 min. To bloom.
  2. Add egg, salt and enough flour to form a soft dough.
  3. Turn on a floured surface. Knead until smooth. 3-5 min.
  4. Do not let dough rest. Divide dough into 12 pieces. Shape each piece into a ball. Place 3" apart on baking sheet ( I eggwashed mine & added seeds).
  5. Cover and let rise 10 min.
  6. Bake at 425° . 8-12 min. Until golden brown. Remove from pan to cooling rack. Let cool.
