The ingredients needed to make Warm green salad and poached egg:
  1. Take 4 eggs
  2. Take 2 ciabatta rolls
  3. Take 200 g any greens (baby marrow, asparagus or mange tout)
  4. Get 1 Tablespoon olive oil
  5. Get 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  6. Prepare 1 Tablespoon honey
  7. Take 1 teaspoon whole grain mustard
  8. Prepare Salami

Instructions to make Warm green salad and poached egg:
  1. Roast 200g baby marrow or any other greens (I finish it in the air fryer to crisp them)
  2. Grease a muffin pan, add half a teaspoon of water and an egg and bake at 180 degrees for 8 minutes
  3. Mix a tablespoon of olive oil, a tablespoon of balsamic vinegar, a tablespoon of honey and a teaspoon of whole grain mustard. Pour a bit over toasted rolls
  4. Add some salami on the rolls and the crispy roasted greens on top of that
  5. Serve the egg on top, with the remaining sauce
