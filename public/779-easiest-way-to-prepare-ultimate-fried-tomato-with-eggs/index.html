The ingredients needed to make Fried Tomato with eggs:
  1. Prepare 4 medium tomatoes
  2. Take 4 eggs
  3. Make ready 2 cloves garlic
  4. Take 1/2 cup water
  5. Make ready as needed oil
  6. Get to taste salt
  7. Make ready to taste sugar

Add the garlic and saute just until it begins to be fragrant. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, a pinch each of salt, black pepper, and white pepper. In a large nonstick or cast-iron skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add whisked eggs and use a spatula.

Steps to make Fried Tomato with eggs:
  1. Cut tomatoes into pieces. Put a tablespoon of oil. When hot, put garlic in and fry.
  2. Put tomatoes into the pot. Fry a bit then add 1 tablespoon of water and put on the lid. (Medium fire) to cook around 5 minutes to let the tomatoes soft. Add a teaspoon of salt and a teaspoon of sugar.(depends on your taste)
  3. Beat the eggs and put into the pot. If you want the eggs solid, let sit for a while. If you want the eggs scrambled, keep on stirring.
