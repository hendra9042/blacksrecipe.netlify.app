Steps to make Poached Egg in Grilled Onion Bowl:
  1. To start with peel the onion, cut the top and make a hole at the centre.
  2. Marinate the onion with salt and black pepper powder.
  3. Heat a grill pan. Add 2 tsp oil and spread all over. Place the onion on it and grill it both the sides for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Next beat the egg into the onion hole. Sprinkle salt and black pepper powder. Add chopped tomato and green chilli. Cover it and cook for 10 minutes or until done over a low flame.
  5. Remove and transfer it to a plate. Thus ready is the delicious Poached Egg in Grilled Onion Bowl for being served.
