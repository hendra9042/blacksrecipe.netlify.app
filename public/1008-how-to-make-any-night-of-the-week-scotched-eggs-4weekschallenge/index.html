The ingredients needed to make Scotched eggs #4weekschallenge:
  1. Make ready 4 pre boiled eggs
  2. Prepare 4 sausages
  3. Make ready Cooking oil for deep frying
  4. Make ready Seasoned bread crumbs
  5. Get 2 beaten eggs
  6. Prepare 1/2 (1 cup) all purpose flour

Instructions to make Scotched eggs #4weekschallenge:
  1. Remove the outer skin of the sausages
  2. Remove the shells from the preparations boiled eggs
  3. Take three bowls in one put your flour, in another put the beaten eggs and in the last one put the seasoned bread crumbs
  4. Take the sausages and roll them using all rolling pin the way you roll when making chapati
  5. Wrap the eggs using the sausages to make a ball
  6. Deep the rolled eggs in the flour first dusting off excess flour, then in the eggs then the bread crumbs dusting off excess crumbs then you can alternatively deep it once more in the eggs to cover them
  7. Let your cooking oil heat then deep fry them
  8. Enjoy
