The ingredients needed to make Ramen la Ruru:
  1. Prepare 1 cup noodles
  2. Take 200 grams chicken kebab or something similer
  3. Make ready 2 stick spring onions
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Take 1/4 Chinese cabbage
  6. Make ready 1 Basil leaves
  7. Prepare 1 chili sauce
  8. Make ready 1 slice lemon

Instructions to make Ramen la Ruru:
  1. In a pen put the chicken kebab and fry it.
  2. Put in a pot 300- 400 ml. water to boil. Then put the cup noodles and fallow the instruction on the noodles package. (add the spices from the noodle cup package and mix.)
  3. Now the kebab is ready leave it aside and put the egg into the boiling noodles for max 2min. Or boil the egg separate.
  4. Chop the Chinese cabbage in tiny slices.
  5. Now remove the boiling noodles away.
  6. Take deep large plate put the noodles in then the cabbage and in the end the meat. Add chili sauce over the meat and the lemon over the cabbage you can add any kind of green goodies. Bon Apetii!
