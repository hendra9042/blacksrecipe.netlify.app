The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Rolls:
  1. Get 4 eggs
  2. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 57 grams butter
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup oil
  5. Make ready 4 cup warm milk
  6. Take 2 tbsp yeast
  7. Take 9 cups flour
  8. Prepare Melted butter
  9. Prepare Brown sugar
  10. Get Cinnamon

Instructions to make Cinnamon Rolls:
  1. Mix eggs, sugar, salt, butter, oil and warm milk
  2. Stir in yeast and let sit for 5 minutes
  3. Add four cups of flour at first, gradually add last four cups
  4. Let dough rest for an hour.
  5. Cut dough in half and roll out one half
  6. Apply melted butter over the rectangular dough and add brown sugar and cinnamon on top, then roll and cut into pieces
  7. Let rest for an hour
  8. Place in baking tray and bake at 175° for 20-25 minutes
