Instructions to make My Bacon,Tom,Cheese+Poached Egg Muffin ðŸ¥°#Breakfast:
  1. Add the bacon and sliced tomato under the grill to cook with sliced tomato
  2. Cut in 2 halves Lightly toast the inside of the Muffin and butter it.
  3. When you turn the bacon and tomatoes over in the grill add a little water to saucepan bring to the boil and add the egg to poach.
  4. Grate the cheese.
  5. Add the cooked bacon to the bottom bun next add the Tomatoes, then add the cheese and press down gently so the egg will sit nice on top.
  6. Next add the egg and then add the top press down lightly so the yolk covers the cheese.
  7. Serve hot and Enjoy
