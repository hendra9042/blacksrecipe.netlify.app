The ingredients needed to make Scotch Eggs:

Steps to make Scotch Eggs:
  1. Combine sausage, sage, parsley and thyme. Pat into 8 rounds.
  2. Cover each egg with a round and roll in seasoned flour, then in beaten egg and bread crumbs.
  3. Deep fry for 10 minutes until sausage is cooked.
  4. Serve with cheese sauce or hollandaise sauce. Can make the night before and fry when you want to eat them.
