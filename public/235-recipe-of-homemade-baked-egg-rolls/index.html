Steps to make Baked egg rolls:
  1. Pre-heat oven to 180°C. In large oven pan add oil or spray oil or parchment paper
  2. In blender add the eggs, milk, flour, salt and pepper. Mix them all.
  3. Pour the mixture into the oven pan
  4. Chop any vegetables as desired
  5. Add the chopped vegetables, cheese and ham or beef on top of the mixture
  6. Cook in oven for 10-15 minutes
  7. Leave the pan for 5 minutes to cool down then start rolling the egg mixtures
