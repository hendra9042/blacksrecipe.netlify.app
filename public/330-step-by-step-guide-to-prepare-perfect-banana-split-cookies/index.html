The ingredients needed to make Banana Split Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup unsalted butter - softened
  2. Take 1 cup packed brown sugar
  3. Get 3 medium very ripe bananas - mashed
  4. Prepare 2 eggs
  5. Get 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  6. Get 2 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 1/4 tsp ground allspice
  9. Get 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  10. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Make ready 1 cup (heaping) semi sweet chocolate chips
  12. Prepare Optional Add-ins
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
  14. Take 1/4 cup chopped dried cherries

Steps to make Banana Split Cookies:
  1. In a large bowl cream together butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed banana until thoroughly combined.
  2. In a separate bowl whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, all spice, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients. Stir well to combine, just until no dry clumps remain. Be careful to not over mix.
  4. Gently fold in chocolate chips and any other optional add-ins you choose. Cover dough and refrigerate 1-2 hours.
  5. When dough is chilled preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly grease a cookie sheet, or line with parchment paper.
  6. Use a couple teaspoons to drop 12 small balls of dough onto cookie sheet, evenly spaced. Bake 8-10 minutes or until cookies are golden.
  7. Repeat steps 6 and 7 until all cookies are baked. This recipe will yield 36-48 cookies depending on the size you choose to make them.
  8. Remove from oven. Let cool on cookie sheet for 2 minutes. Transfer cookies to wire racks to cool completely.
