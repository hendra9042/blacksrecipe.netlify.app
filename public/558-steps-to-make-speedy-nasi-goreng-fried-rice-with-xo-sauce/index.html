The ingredients needed to make Nasi Goreng Fried Rice (with XO sauce):
  1. Get 1.5 tbsp oil
  2. Make ready 3 cups cooked rice (cold, 1 day old)
  3. Get 2 tbsp kecap manis
  4. Take 1 tbsp XO sauce
  5. Make ready 1 small onion diced
  6. Take 2 garlic cloves chopped
  7. Take 1 fried egg (optional topping)
  8. Prepare 1-2 stalks green onion diced

Steps to make Nasi Goreng Fried Rice (with XO sauce):
  1. Heat oil in a large wok/pan
  2. Add garlic, stir fry until golden brown
  3. Add onion, stir fry until brown
  4. Add the rice and the kecap manis. Continue to stir fry until rice is caramelized.
  5. Add the xo sauce - make sure to get some chilli and dried shrimp pieces
  6. Continue to stir fry until rice is completely covered and caramelized.
  7. Remove the rice from the pan.
  8. You can optionally add 1 tsp of oil and fry an egg.
