Steps to make Egg fried noodles:
  1. Cut all the vegetables in juline style
  2. In a pan add water and salt and boil the egg noodles, strain and rinse with cold water and set aside
  3. In a bowl add eggs and season with salt and pepper and mix
  4. In a wok heat 1 tbsp of oil and cook egg till srumble
  5. When egg are fully cooked with in a corner add remaining oil and add chopped garlic and saute for 2 minutes
  6. Add all the vegetables and spices and sauce and saute for 5 minutes
  7. Add cooked noodles give a good mix and some more spring onion
  8. Serve with chicken wonton
