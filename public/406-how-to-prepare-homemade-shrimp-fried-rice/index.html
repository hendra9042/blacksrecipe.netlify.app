Steps to make Shrimp fried-rice:
  1. Mix some salt and peppercorns and rub along the shrimp well. Put some oil in a pan and lightly saute the shrimp.
  2. Parboil the rice until its 80% done and keep aside.
  3. Put all the veggies together in pan and and lightly saute them. Add the garam masala and bay leaf and soy-sauce and continue stirring in between. Cover and cook till veggies are tender.
