Steps to make Breakfast Idea: poached egg with baked tomatoes üçÖ:
  1. Mince the garlic. In an oven proof dish add the tomatoes. Pour over the balsamic vinegar, oil, paprika and minced garlic. Season with salt and pepper and sprinkle some sugar over the tomatoes.
  2. Turn the oven to 180degrees and bake the tomatoes for 10 minutes.
  3. Boil the water in a pan. Add some salt, a little oil and the malt vinegar. Bring to a rolling boil.
  4. When the water is boiling use a spatula to stir the water around the edge of the pan. Crack the egg in the centre to poach it. Cook for 2-4 minutes depending on how well done you like your eggs.
  5. Wilt the spinach in a small pan with a little oil, juice from the lemon wedge and salt and pepper. Add a little water to the spinach to steam it.
  6. Toast and butter your bread. Top with the wilted spinach, roast tomatoes and poached egg. Season with salt and pepper and sprinkle the sunflower seeds on top. Enjoy!!
