Instructions to make Chocolate Keto Chaffles:
  1. Preheat 4 in waffle maker
  2. Put all chaffle ingredients in blender and mix or in bowl and mix until well mixed.
  3. Spray waffle maker with olive oil or brush with melted butter.
  4. Ladle in enough chaffle batter to cover bottom of waffle maker. (Experience will show how much.)
  5. Bake four minutes or a little longer if you want crisper chaffles.
  6. Put on rack to cool.
  7. Mix topping ingredients and spread on cooled chaffles.
  8. Chaffles can be frozen and be thawed or microwaved later.
