The ingredients needed to make Mixed Beans + Chickpea and Veg Medley with Avocado and Paprika Poached Egg-(Cupboard Raid Dinner):
  1. Prepare 1 Tin or 400g of Mixed Beans
  2. Make ready 1 Small Tin or 210g of Chickpea
  3. Get 1/2 Green Bell Pepper(Sliced)
  4. Make ready 1 Large Red Onions(Sliced)
  5. Take 2 Jumbo Mushrooms(Sliced)
  6. Take 1 Medium Sized Leeks(Chopped)
  7. Get 1 Tsp Smoked Paprika and Dried Chives
  8. Take 40 g Fresh Marjoram Leaves
  9. Prepare 2 Tbsp Tomato Pesto
  10. Take 1 Tsp Light Soy Sauce
  11. Take Seasoning (Salt&Pepper)
  12. Make ready 1 Avocado
