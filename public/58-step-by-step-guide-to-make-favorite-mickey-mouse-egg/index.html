Instructions to make Mickey Mouse Egg:
  1. Bisect egg vertically. Mark with marker.
  2. Break shell so that its big enough to remove the egg inside without breaking the yolk
  3. Remove the egg and separate the yolk and white
  4. Cut a wider opening in the shell near the marked line and pour the egg white back in slowly
  5. Line a towel under the lid of your steamer so that the moisture doesn't get into the egg white. Steam the egg for 13 mins.
  6. Use your cutter and cut out the mickey face.
  7. Beat up the yolk and use a 1/4 teaspoon to put some yolk into the hole. Need to be really careful since it's so small. If there are bubbles, pop them with a toothpick.
  8. Wrap the ramekin with some plastic wrap and re-steam the egg for another 3 mins.
