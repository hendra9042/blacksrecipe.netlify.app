The ingredients needed to make Poached eggs with garlicky sprouts:
  1. Get 1 tbsp grass fed butter or ghee
  2. Make ready 2 cup brussel sprouts, sliced
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  5. Prepare 4 eggs
  6. Prepare 1 black sea salt to garnish (optional)
  7. Get 2 tsp garlic powder

Instructions to make Poached eggs with garlicky sprouts:
  1. In a skillet over medium heat, warm the butter
  2. Add the brussel sprouts to the skillet, and sauté
  3. Sprinkle the brussel sprouts with garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Continue to sauté until the sprouts are tender.
  4. Heat some water in an egg poacher. Crack the eggs into the poaching cups, cover with a lid, and cook for 4 minutes
  5. Plate the brussel sprouts, top them with poached eggs, and sprinkle with black sea salt
