  4. Prepare 1/2 red onion
  5. Prepare 5 garlic cloves
  6. Get 5 bird eye chili
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp tomato paste
  8. Prepare 1 egg
  9. Take 2 spring onion
  10. Get 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  11. Get 2 tbsp canola oil

Steps to make Spicy Fried Rice with Chili:
  1. Cut up coysum, spring onion, and meat balls
  2. Chop onion, garlic, and chilies
  3. Heat up oil in a frying pan, add chopped ingredients mix them for 5 mins.
  4. Crack the egg and add it into frying pan.
  5. Add cut meat balls and coysum, stir them for 5 mins. Add salt and mix it properly.
  6. Add rice, mix them well.
  7. Add sweet soy sauce, mix them well.
  8. Add cut spring onion, mix them well. Taste the salt.
  9. Move it into plates and enjoy it with crackers or anything you like.
