Instructions to make Perfectly Boiled Omega-3 Eggs:
  1. Put the eggs into a small cooking pot.
  2. Pour all the fresh water into the cooking pot until all the eggs are submerged completely. Then cover it.
  3. Turn on the stove with small flame.
  4. Put the cooking pot on the stove and wait about 15-20 mins until it boils.
  5. When it starts boiling, turn it off immediately and wait until the water inside become cooler (around 15 mins) while the pot is still covered.
  6. When the water inside becomes cooler, pick the eggs and it's all done.
