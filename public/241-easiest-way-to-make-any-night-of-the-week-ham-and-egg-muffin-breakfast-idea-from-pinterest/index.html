Steps to make Ham and Egg Muffin- Breakfast Idea From PInterest:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. sautee chopped onion on the skillet in olive oil
  3. spray cooking oil on to cupcakepan ( I only made two so I only sprayed to cups)
  4. add one slice of ham to each cup
  5. whisk one egg on a bowl and do the same with the other egg
  6. add the whisked eggs to the ham
  7. add cooked onion/cilantro/cheese/ black pepper and salt to cup and mix carefully with a fork
  8. bake for 20 -25minutes and enjoy
