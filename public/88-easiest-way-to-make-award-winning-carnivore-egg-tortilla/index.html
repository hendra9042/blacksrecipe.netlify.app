The ingredients needed to make Carnivore Egg tortilla:
  1. Prepare 5 Hard boiled eggs,
  2. Get 3 Raw eggs,
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Bacon grease or other for cooking
  4. Take Salt to taste
  5. Get Black pepper to taste

Instructions to make Carnivore Egg tortilla:
  1. Chop hard boiled eggs in mediums sized chunks
  2. Heat frying pan on stove top. Once very hot, add bacon grease (or other cooking oil).
  3. Mix the chopped hard boiled eggs wirh 3 eggs. Salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Place in frying pan on high flame. After 2 minutes, lower to medium flame and let cook around 8 minutes.
  5. Check bottom of tortilla. Once the bottom is golden and the tortilla is firm, cover the frying pan with big plate and rapidly flip, so you can slide the uncooked side back to the frying pan.
  6. Let cook until bottom is golden.
  7. Remove from stove top. Let cool slightly.
