The ingredients needed to make Magic almond rolls:
  1. Make ready 3 cups sifted bread flour
  2. Take 1 teaspoons salt
  3. Prepare 1/2 cups butter
  4. Get 1/4 cups milk
  5. Make ready 1/4 cups hot water
  6. Get 2 packs yeast
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon sugar
  8. Take 3 eggs
  9. Take 1 teaspoons vanilla
  10. Make ready 1 cups chopped almonds
  11. Prepare 1/2 cups sugar

Instructions to make Magic almond rolls:
  1. To 11/2 cups flour add salt and butter blend well as for a pie crust
  2. Dissolve yeast in water and milk warmed to about 110 degrees
  3. Add to flour beat until smooth
  4. Cover let stand for 20 minutes
  5. Add beaten eggs beat vigorously fold in remaining flour
  6. Add vanilla stir till smooth
  7. Tie dough loosely in cheese cloth
  8. Drop into pan of warm water let rise for 1 hour
  9. Roll out to about 1/2 inch
  10. Cut into strips shape into figure 8s
  11. Roll in sugar and almonds let stand 5. Minutes
  12. Bake at 425 on well greased sheet pan for 15 minutes
