The ingredients needed to make Special fried rice with curry sauce - UK chip shop style:
  1. Prepare 4 portions white long grain rice or Jasmin rice. Cooked and cooled
  2. Prepare 6 eggs
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  4. Take 3 tbsp veg oil
  5. Prepare 150 gr frozen garden peas
  6. Take 150 gr little prawns
  7. Get 300 gr steamed chicken breast
  8. Make ready 300 gr red Chinese pork - see my other recipe
  9. Prepare Spring onion
  10. Prepare Curry sauce and prawn crackers to serve. (Optional)
  11. Take 1 white onion diced

Steps to make Special fried rice with curry sauce - UK chip shop style:
  1. In a hot frying pan add the oil and fry the onion until soft but not brown. Add 2 eggs and scrambled
  2. Remove from the heat and add the rice, soy sauce, cooked prawns and defrosted peas.
  3. In a bowl or plastic container slice a cooked chicken breast and some red pork. Then fry one more egg like an omelet and fold in half. Put on top.
  4. Put the rice mixture on top of the egg and slightly press down. Cover then i heat in microwave for 1 min on high - turn upside down on a warmed plate or put in fridge for 2-3 days to eat later.
  5. Eat with prawn crackers and so Chinese curry sauce (easy to buy at most super markets. Just as water a 1 Tbsp sugar). ENJOY ;-)
