The ingredients needed to make Fried Green Beans with Scrumbled Eggs:
  1. Make ready 1/2 oz green beans
  2. Prepare 3 clove onion, chopped
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Take 1 tsp salt
  5. Get 1/2 tsp sugar
  6. Make ready 3 tbsp margarine for scrambled eggs
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp margarine to fry

Steps to make Fried Green Beans with Scrumbled Eggs:
  1. Diagnonally cut all the green beans into quite thin pieces. Chop the onions. Set both aside.
  2. Prepare to heat a frying pan. Add 3 spoons of margarine into the pan. Add the eggs and scramble them. Set aside.
  3. Add another 3 spoons of margarine to the frying pan you just use to cook the scrambled eggs. Fry the onions for 2 minutes. Add the beans and fry for 3 minutes. Add the salt and sugar, steer. Add the eggs.
