The ingredients needed to make Egg Salad:
  1. Take 8 large eggs
  2. Take 1/4 cup mayo
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup dill minced
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp chives minced
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  6. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Take 1/4 tsp pepper
