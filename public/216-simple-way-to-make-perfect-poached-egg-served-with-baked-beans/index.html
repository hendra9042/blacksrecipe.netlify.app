Instructions to make Poached Egg served with baked beans:
  1. Add Ketchup, berbeque sauce, blackpepper, chilli and garlic salt in a bowl.
  2. Add beans and toss up well using to mix
  3. Add strips of bacon and sausages and cheese.
  4. In a preheated oven at 350f/180 c. Cover and bake for 30 minutes.
  5. For the poached egg boil water in pan. Add salt to it and break your egg on the water
  6. Cook egg on medium heat to avoid it breaking off. Cook it for about 5 minutes
  7. Serve with Toasted bread for breakfast.
