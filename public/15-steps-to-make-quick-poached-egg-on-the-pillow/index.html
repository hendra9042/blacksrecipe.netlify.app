The ingredients needed to make Poached Egg on the Pillow:
  1. Make ready Water
  2. Get 1 large egg
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon rice vinegar or other mild-tasting vinegar (5%)
  4. Take to taste Salt, pepper
  5. Make ready 1 piece bread (any that you like)
  6. Take 5-10 leaves spinach
  7. Make ready 3-5 cherry tomatoes

Steps to make Poached Egg on the Pillow:
  1. Fill the saucepan with water about 2/3 full and bring to a boil
  2. Once water is boiling, turn the heat down (bubbles should be coming up, but it won't be rolling)
  3. Wash the egg and crack it into a small cup
  4. Add vinegar to the water
  5. Lower the egg into the water and then tip it out into the water
  6. Cook for 4 minutes
  7. Meanwhile prepare a “pillow” for your poached egg: on a piece of bread (you can slightly fry/toast it) put spinach and chopped tomatoes
  8. Fill a small bowl with cold water
  9. Remove the egg from the saucepan and put it into cold water for 1 minute
  10. Dry the egg with a paper towel
  11. Place poached egg on the top of the “pillow” (on top of tomatoes)
  12. Season with salt and pepper
