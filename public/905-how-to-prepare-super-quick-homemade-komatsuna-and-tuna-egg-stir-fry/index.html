  2. Take 1 can Canned tuna
  3. Get 1 to 2 Egg
  4. Get 1 pack Bonito flakes
  5. Get 1 dash Salt and pepper
  6. Get 2 tsp Soy sauce
  7. Take 1 tsp Mayonnaise
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp Sesame oil
