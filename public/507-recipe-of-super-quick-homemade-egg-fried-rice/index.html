The ingredients needed to make Egg Fried rice:
  1. Get 2 big capsicum chopped
  2. Take 3 big bunch of spring onion chopped
  3. Prepare 3 big carrots (grated)
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 cup green peas
  5. Take 10-12 green chillies
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp soya sauce
  7. Prepare to taste Salt
  8. Take 7-8 eggs
  9. Prepare 1 tsp red chilli powder
  10. Make ready 1 cup rice
  11. Get as needed Oil
  12. Get 1 tbsp jeera

Instructions to make Egg Fried rice:
  1. Chop all the vegiees
  2. Take a bowl add eggs,red chilli powder and salt mix well..take a pan add oil and cook eggs and crumbled it..keep aside
  3. Now take a big vessel add oil,jeera than slowly keep on adding all the vegiee,soya sauce and salt.. cook till it's compltly done
  4. Now add crumbled eggs to the cooked vegiees..mix it well
  5. Now cook rice and the mixture to it and mix it very lightly..keep on very low flame till 15 mins..and serve..
  6. You can also add boiled shreded chicken to it..just boil chicken in ginger garlic paste and salt.. shred it and mix into vegiess once vegiees are cooked well..
