  1. Get 2 tbsp strawberries, chopped

  5. Prepare as required Chocolate syrup

Instructions to make Nutella French toast rolls:
  1. Take a pan add 1 tsp butter and add apple strawberries and pineapple and stir cook and keep aside.
  2. Grind cinnamon stick and make fine powder keep aside.
  3. Take 2 tbsp sugar powder and mix well with 1 tsp cinnamon powder.
  4. Take eggs and add 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder mix well and beat.
  5. Take bread slices and spread nutella and keep fruits and fold dip in egg batter and take a pan add butter and stir fry rolls.
  6. Put in a plate and spread sugar-cinnamon powder Nutella spread and garnish with chocolate syrup and decorated with gems.
  7. Enjoy!
