The ingredients needed to make Sandwich Rolls:
  1. Make ready 2 tablespoons butter melted
  2. Make ready 2 tablespoons yeast
  3. Make ready As needed oil for coating bowl and dough
  4. Take 5 cups all purpose flour for rolls
  5. Prepare 1 cup water tap water hot
  6. Prepare 1-1/2 teaspoon salt
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoons sugar
  8. Get 1/4 cup flour for dusting
  9. Take 1 cup evaporated milk
  10. Get 2 tablespoons water for egg wash
  11. Take 1 large egg for egg wash

Instructions to make Sandwich Rolls:
  1. Add yeast to hot tap water. Add the butter, milk, salt, and sugar and whisk it good. Stir in the flour with the whisk. Keep adding flour. Knead till its completely mixed.
  2. Form a ball. Oil a heat safe bowl. Coat the dough ball with oil. Set in a warm place. We have a bread proofing setting on our stove. Let it rise for one hour or till it doubles in size. After an hour poke it with your hands.
  3. Knead 3-5 times. Let rest 5 minutes. I cut mine into 2 strips then made 5 cuts across.
  4. Separate the cuts my last cut pair was small so I combined them together giving me 9 portions. Form into ball and place on a baking pan with parchment paper. Let them sit covered with paper towels for 20 minutes.
  5. Mix the egg and water. Brush the egg wash over the rolls. Place in the oven for 16-20 minutes. Take off the hot pan and let cool. I hope you enjoy.
