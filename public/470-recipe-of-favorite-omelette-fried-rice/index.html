Steps to make Omelette Fried rice:
  1. Sabse pahle ek bowl m egg tordker daliye.
  2. Usi bowl m sabhi chopped veges daliye. Agar aap chaho to hari mirch bhi dal sakte ho.

  4. Ab tawa garam ker ke oil daliye.
