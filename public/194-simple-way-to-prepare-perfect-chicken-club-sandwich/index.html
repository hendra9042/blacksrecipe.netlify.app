Instructions to make Chicken Club Sandwich:
  1. Wash and drain chicken mince.
  2. Combine all the ingredients with the mince and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Heat oil in nonstick pan. - Take about 1-1/2 tbsp of mince and roll it the panko breadcrumbs. Shape it into large-sized patty and shallow fry in medium hot oil. Drain excess oil on paper towels.
  4. Fry eggs in the remaining oil or add more as required.
  5. To assemble: - - Apply mayonnaise to bread slices. Layer them with cheese and salad leaves.
  6. Place the chicken patty on bottom layer and the egg on the top layer of the sandwich.
