The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Paste Fried Rice:
  1. Take Cooking oil
  2. Prepare 1/2 onion, diced
  3. Prepare 3 cups rice, cooked
  4. Get 1 clove garlic, minced
  5. Take 1 tbsp shrimp paste or substitute
  6. Prepare Salt to taste
  7. Get 1/2 oz cooking wine
  8. Take 3/4 cup soy meat, diced
  9. Make ready 2 large eggs, scrambled
  10. Take 1 tsp brown sugar
  11. Make ready 2 tsp lemon or lime juice
  12. Take 1.5 cups lettuce, shredded
