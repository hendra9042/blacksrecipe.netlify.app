The ingredients needed to make Salted Fish and Chicken Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 chicken thighs fillets
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Prepare Salted Fish
  4. Make ready Bean sprouts
  5. Prepare 1 Spring onion
  6. Get 1 Brown onion
  7. Get 1 tsp Fresh ginger

  10. Take 1 & ½ cups of rice
  11. Take 1 tsp Chinese cooking wine
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
  13. Get 1 tsp Fish sauce
  14. Get 1/2 Tbsp Light soy sauce
  15. Take 1 tsp Sesame oil
  16. Make ready Salt & pepper (for seasoning)
