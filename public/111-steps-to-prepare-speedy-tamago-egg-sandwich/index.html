Instructions to make Tamago (egg) Sandwich:
  1. Boil eggs
  2. After cooked, throw away the egg shell
  3. Split the egg, then put the egg whites and yolk to separate bowl
  4. Mash the egg yolk using a fork or whisk
  5. Cut the egg whites into smaller pieces and mash it as small as possible using a fork or whisk
  6. Mix the egg whites and yolk
  7. Add good amount of mayo (depends on preference, but i prefer about 2 tablespoon) to the egg mix
  8. Add a pinch or two of salt, then mix it until even and soft texture
  9. Spread the mix to the bread, then cut it diagonally
  10. Your tamago sandwich is ready, enjoy!
