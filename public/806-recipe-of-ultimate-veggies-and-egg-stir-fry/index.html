The ingredients needed to make Veggies and egg stir fry:
  1. Prepare 4 tbsp Chopped cabbage
  2. Take 1 medium carrot
  3. Take 3 tbsp Mix frozen vegetables
  4. Prepare 1 large egg
  5. Prepare 1 small onion
  6. Get 1 tsp chopped chilli
  7. Get 2 tbsp capsicum
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  9. Get 1/4 tsp Chopped garlic
  10. Take 1/4 tsp Chopped ginger
  11. Get 2 tbsp Olive oil
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  14. Prepare 1 tsp soy sauce
  15. Prepare to taste Salt

Instructions to make Veggies and egg stir fry:
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat add chopped garlic and ginger,saute a few minutes until fragrant. Add vegetables and carrot, saute until half boil.
  2. Add frozen mix vegetables and cook until they begin to soften,than add capsicum, onion and stir for few minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce and cook a minute more, all vegetables move to one side of pane… another side of pan add 1tbsp olive oil on medium heat,pour egg mixture and Cook for 1minute and stir nicely.
  4. Mix fry vegetables and fry egg gently to combine. sprinkle black pepper and chaat masala,stir for 2 minutes.
  5. Veggies and egg stir fry is ready. garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves.serve with hot rice,roti,parota.
