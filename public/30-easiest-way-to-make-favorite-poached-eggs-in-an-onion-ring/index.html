Steps to make Poached Eggs in an Onion Ring.:
  1. Heat your skillet to medium high heat, cut your onion into large flat sided rings.
  2. Heat a tablespoon of olive oil and place an onion ring in the center of the pan, flatten it as best you can with a spatula.
  3. While holding the onion ring firmly against the pan crack an egg inside of the ring and cover.
  4. Pour a tablespoon of water into the pan and re-cover to allow the steam to cook the top of the eggs.
  5. Season to your liking and enjoy!
