The ingredients needed to make Scotch eggs:

  4. Get 1 cup flour
  5. Get Oil for frying
  6. Take 1 large onion- shredded
  7. Get 1 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 3/4 cup bread crumbs
  9. Make ready 1 tsp garlic pounded
