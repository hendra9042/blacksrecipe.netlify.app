The ingredients needed to make Nigerian egg rolls:
  1. Prepare 250 grams All-purpose flour
  2. Get 2 tsp Yeast
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Ground nutmeg
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp Salt
  5. Make ready 1 Sugar to taste
  6. Take 1 Lukewarm water
  7. Make ready 15 eggs

Steps to make Nigerian egg rolls:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients together
  2. Add water and mix it till it becomes sticky
  3. Set it aside for 1 hour
  4. Boil the eggs for 20 minutes
  5. Wrap the eggs with the dough.
  6. Deep fry them till brown
