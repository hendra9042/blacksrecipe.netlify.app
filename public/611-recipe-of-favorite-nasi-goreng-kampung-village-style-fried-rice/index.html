The ingredients needed to make Nasi Goreng Kampung (Village-Style Fried Rice):
  1. Take 1 plate of cooked rice (preferably cold rice, room temperature)
  2. Prepare 1 egg
  3. Prepare 2 cloves garlic
  4. Make ready 3 shallots
  5. Make ready 1 red chili
  6. Prepare 1 Tbsp kecap manis (sweet soy sauce)
  7. Prepare 1/2 Tbsp soy sauce
  8. Take 1/2 Tsp sugar
  9. Get 1/4 Tsp white pepper
  10. Make ready pinch salt

Steps to make Nasi Goreng Kampung (Village-Style Fried Rice):
  1. Prepare the ingredients. Thinly chop garlic, shallot, and chili
  2. Preheat 3 tbsp oil on medium heat. Whisk the egg and pour it into the pan. Scramble it. Add chili, garlic and shallot. Stir them until fragrant.
  3. Add rice to the pan, then mix it. Put sweet soy sauce, soy sauce, sugar, salt and white pepper. Stir it for about 8 minutes.
  4. Nasi Goreng Kampung is ready to serve with krupuk (shrimp cracker) and sunny side up egg ✨
