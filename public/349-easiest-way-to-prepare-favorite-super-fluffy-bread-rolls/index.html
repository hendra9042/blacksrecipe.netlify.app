The ingredients needed to make Super Fluffy Bread Rolls:
  1. Prepare 280 gr Hi Protein Flour
  2. Get 40 gr Sugar
  3. Prepare 4 gr Instant Yeast
  4. Make ready 180 cc Egg + Milk
  5. Prepare 30 gr Butter
  6. Take 2 gr Salt
  7. Get 1 Egg Yolk + Pinch of Sugar + 1 tsp water for brushing

Instructions to make Super Fluffy Bread Rolls:
  1. In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar and yeast.
  2. Pour in milk and egg mixture and stir until the dough starts to pull together.
  3. Add butter and salt, and knead until smooth
  4. Divide into 12 portions, shape into ball, and put on the pan
  5. Cover with cling wrap loosely, and let it proof for 45-60 min or until double in size
  6. Brush with egg yolk mixture.
  7. Bake for 15-20 minutes @190 degrees C, in the preheated oven until nicely browned on the top and bottom.
