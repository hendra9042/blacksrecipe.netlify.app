The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Peach Yogurt Muffins:
  1. Make ready 1 1/4 cups flour
  2. Take 1/8 tsp salt
  3. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup peach yogurt
  5. Make ready 1 egg
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup butter
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  8. Get 4 large strawberries
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps to make Strawberry Peach Yogurt Muffins:
  1. Cream together the butter and the sugar in a mixer.
  2. Add the egg and mix.
  3. Slowly add the flour, baking powder, and salt alternating with the peach yogurt.
  4. Add lemon juice. Cut the strawberries and fold them in.
  5. Preheat oven to 375
  6. Grease you muffin pan and pour in batter
  7. Bake in oven for 20-25 minutes.
