The ingredients needed to make Egg drop soup:
  1. Prepare 1 cup chicken stock
  2. Get 1 tbsp soy sauce
  3. Get 1/2 tbsp fish sauce
  4. Take pinch fresh ginger
  5. Take 1/4 tsp toasted sesame oil
  6. Get 1 egg
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp corn starch
  8. Take 2 tbsp water
  9. Prepare 1 scallion green part only
  10. Take to taste salt and pepper
