The ingredients needed to make Lumpiang Shanghai (Pork Spring Rolls):
  1. Take 1 kg Ground pork (lean)
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped onion
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped kinchay
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped celery
  6. Get 1 egg
  7. Prepare dash salt and pepper
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup grated cheese
  9. Take 40 lumpia wrapper
  10. Prepare 1 1/2 cup oil (depends on how deep is your pan)
  11. Take 1/4 cup vinegar
  12. Make ready 1/4 cup sugar
  13. Make ready 3 tbsp banana ketchup
  14. Prepare 1/4 cup water

Steps to make Lumpiang Shanghai (Pork Spring Rolls):
  1. Mix all ingredients.
  2. Wrap it.
  3. Heat oil for deep frying. Cook for about 7 - 9 minutes each batch.
  4. Soon as done, drip it. While draining the excess oil make a sauce.
  5. In a sauce pan, mix vinegar, sugar, ketchup , water and salt. Let it boil till it thickens.
  6. Serve with hot rice.
