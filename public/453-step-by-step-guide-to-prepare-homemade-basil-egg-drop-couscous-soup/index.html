The ingredients needed to make Basil egg drop couscous soup:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup Israeli couscous
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 quart water
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp cumino seeds
  4. Take 2 tbsp sesame oil
  5. Make ready 3 tbsp soy sauce
  6. Take 1 tsp basil
  7. Take 2 tbsp roasted sesame seeds
  8. Get 1 tbsp bullion paste
  9. Get 1 tsp ground black pepper
  10. Take 2 eggs
