  1. Prepare 1 TSP cornstarch

  3. Get 1 tsp salt

  5. Get 2 tbsp rice wine
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup cooking oil
  7. Make ready 2 scallions thiny sliced
  8. Prepare 2 medium size tomatoes, roughly chopped
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp ketchup
  10. Take 1/4 tspsugar

Steps to make Stir fried egg and tomatoes:
  1. In small bowl stir cornstarch with 2 tbsp water set a side
  2. In a medium mixing bowl beat the egg with salt and white pepper add the rice wine
  3. Heat 3 tbsp oil in the high heat wok stir fry scallion about 20 second add the egg stir fry until everything eventually cooked and just tender transfer to a plate n set a side
  4. Add the remaining oil add chopped tomatoes to the wok fry until most all of tomatoes has cooked down add the ketchup,sugar, and cornstarch stir around until the mixture has thickened slightly
  5. Add the egg to the wok and stir fry until mixed
  6. Enjoy
