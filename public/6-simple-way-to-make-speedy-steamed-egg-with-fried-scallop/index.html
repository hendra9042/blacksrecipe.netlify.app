The ingredients needed to make Steamed Egg with Fried Scallop:
  1. Get 6 pieces dried scallops
  2. Take 3 large white eggs
  3. Make ready 1 stalk spring.onion
  4. Make ready 1 tsp oyster sauce,diluted in water
  5. Take 1 tsp cooking oil
