Steps to make Jammy soft boiled eggs:
  1. Place some water in a pot.
  2. Take the eggs out of the fridge and leave it out.
  3. Set the temperature to below max. I set my stove top to be 8 which is one right below the max.
  4. Watch the water - you want the water to start simmering just a bit (small bubbles) - not to a full boil - this is to prevent the egg shells from cracking.
  5. Place the eggs into the pot and boil them for 6 and half minutes (6 mins 30 seconds). Do not lower the temperature.
  6. When the timer goes off, place the eggs in cold water and drop a couple of ice cubes (ice bath) for a couple of minutes. The ice bath will stop the eggs from cooking and also it will help with the peeling.
  7. Peel the eggs and enjoy.
