Instructions to make Hubby’s Chinese fried rice:
  1. Pour some water into a clean pot,add a little Tumeric, add 3 bay leaf to the water and allow it to boil before adding rice to it.
  2. Once the rice is Parboiled, set aside the rice and put your pot on the fire before adding some butter to it.
  3. Pour your veggies into the pot, add Maggi cubes, powdered garlic and fry the veggies until well fried.
  4. Get a pan and add a little butter to it, before adding your minced meat to it.fry your minced meat until it becomes a little bit brown
  5. Add your minced meat to the fried veggies and set aside before you put back your pot on fire and also add some butter to it break your eggs into the empty pot and make the egg in a scrambled way before pouring the fried veggies to it.
  6. 6-add your rice to the veggies and turn until all the spices and veggies are mixed well with the rice before lower your fire and allow the rice for like 3 minutes before you bring it down and serve.
