The ingredients needed to make Healthy Blueberry Muffins:
  1. Get flour
  2. Get baking soda
  3. Make ready salt
  4. Get ground cinnamon
  5. Make ready honey
  6. Make ready brown sugar, loosely packed
  7. Get mashed very ripe banana
  8. Prepare plain yogurt
  9. Prepare large egg, beaten
  10. Take milk
  11. Get blueberries

Instructions to make Healthy Blueberry Muffins:
    1. Preheat oven to 350°. Spray muffin tin with nonstick spray.
  2. Gently whisk flour, soda, salt and cinnamon in large bowl.
  3. In another bowl, mix together honey and brown sugar. Get out as many lumps as possible. Stir in banana, yogurt and egg.
  4. Pour wet ingredients into dry and mix together. Stir in milk slowly. Gently fold in blueberries.
  5. Fill muffin tin 1/2-3/4 way full with mixture.
  6. Bake for 17-18 minutes or until very light brown on edges. Cool 5 minutes before moving to wire rack to cool completely. Makes 12 muffins.
