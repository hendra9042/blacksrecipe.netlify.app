The ingredients needed to make Scrambled egg with potato and sausages:
  1. Make ready 1 Tbsp. butter
  2. Prepare 2 cups Potatoes
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup chopped onions
  4. Make ready 1 small jalapeño pepper, finely chopped
  5. Get 6 eggs
  6. Prepare Salt and pepper to taste
  7. Get 1 small green pepper diced
  8. Take 1 cup sausages

Steps to make Scrambled egg with potato and sausages:
  1. Melt butter in large nonstick skillet on medium heat. Add potatoes, onions and jalapeño peppers; cook 7 to 8 min. or until onions are tender and potatoes are golden brown, stirring frequently.
  2. Add sausages and green pepper.
  3. Beat eggs and salt, black pepper in small bowl; add to skillet. Cook 3 min. or until eggs are set, stirring occasionally
