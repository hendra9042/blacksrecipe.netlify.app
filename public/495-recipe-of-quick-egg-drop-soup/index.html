The ingredients needed to make Egg drop soup:
  1. Take 3 cup Chicken broth
  2. Take 2 Beaten eggs
  3. Get to taste Salt
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp Chilli flakes half teaspoon
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Soya sauce

Instructions to make Egg drop soup:
  1. Boil chicken broth in a pan.
  2. Add salt and chilli flakes in it. Mix well.
  3. Slowly pour the beaten eggs in it. Stir continuesely.
  4. The egg will spread out into ribbons.
  5. Add soya sauce in it. Mix well.
  6. Switch off the flame. It's ready.
