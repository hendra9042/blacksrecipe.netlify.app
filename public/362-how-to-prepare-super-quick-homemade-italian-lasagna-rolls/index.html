Steps to make Italian Lasagna Rolls:
  1. Boil lasagna in pot of salted water until aldente (cooked but firm)
  2. Mix all other ingredients in a large bowl(except sauce)
  3. Put a ladle of sauce spread out on the bottom of a baking tray.
  4. Put a healthy scoop of cheese mix at one end of a lasagna strand & roll.
  5. Fill the tray with rolls & ladle some sauce over the rolls & sprinkle some shredded mozzarella & grated cheese over the rolls.
  6. Bake in 425° pretty heated oven until top slightly browns.
  7. Remove from oven. Let sit & firm a bit & serve.
