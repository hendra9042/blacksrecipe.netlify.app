The ingredients needed to make Soft Poached egg smashed avocado grilled tomato on sourdough:
  1. Make ready 2 avocados
  2. Prepare 2 eggs
  3. Take 1 Lime juiced
  4. Prepare 2 slices sourdough
  5. Make ready 1 tomato
  6. Make ready 100 g Rockets
  7. Get pinch Salt
  8. Make ready pinch Pepper
  9. Make ready 400 ml Water
  10. Get 2 tablespoon Viniger

  2. Using a fork crush avocado with lime juice season with salt and pepper
  3. Grill sourdough and tomato.
  4. Smooth avocado onto sourdough top with your poached egg add some rocket and your grilled tomato
