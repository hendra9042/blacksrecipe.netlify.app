The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Yin Yang Honeymoon Fried Rice:
  1. Take 2 cups rice, cooked
  2. Make ready 3 eggs, scrambled
  3. Take Cooking oil
  4. Prepare Béchamel sauce
  5. Get 1 tbsp butter
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp flour
  7. Prepare 2 cups milk
  8. Take 1/2 carrot, diced
  9. Take 3/4 cup peas
  10. Prepare 10-12 pieces soy chicken
  11. Get 1/3 cup stock
  12. Get Tomato sauce
  13. Take 2 tomatoes, quartered
  14. Prepare 1/2 shallot, sliced
  15. Get 2 oz vegetarian ham
  16. Make ready 2 garlic cloves, minced
  17. Prepare 1 tbsp tomato paste
  18. Get 3/4 water
  19. Take Salt to taste
  20. Get Dash white pepper
  21. Make ready 2 tsp cornstarch
  22. Prepare 1 oz cilantro, chopped (optional)

Instructions to make Vegetarian Yin Yang Honeymoon Fried Rice:
  1. In a pan heat oil and mix eggs and cooked rice together. Scoop into serving dish. Set aside.
  2. In a sauce pan heat oil, garlic, tomatoes paste, tomatoes, salt, pepper, and shallots. Add water and cornstarch. Cook until sauce thickens and tomatoes softens. Pour onto one side of rice.
  3. In a sauce pan melt butter and stir in flour. Add stock, milk, peas, carrots, and chicken. Mix well. Cook until sauce thickens. Pour onto other half of rice. Garnish with cilantro.
