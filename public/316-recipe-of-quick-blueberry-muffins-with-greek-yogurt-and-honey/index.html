The ingredients needed to make Blueberry muffins with Greek yogurt and honey:
  1. Prepare 2 cups whole wheat flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  3. Take 1/2 cup + 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  4. Get 1 tablespoon baking powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
  7. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  8. Make ready 1 cup light and fit vanilla Greek yogurt
  9. Make ready 1/2 cup whole milk
  10. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  11. Prepare 2 large eggs room temperature
  12. Take 1/4 cup honey
  13. Take 1/2 cup olive oil
  14. Prepare 2 cups blueberries

Steps to make Blueberry muffins with Greek yogurt and honey:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Line 12 cup muffin tin with paper liners and set aside
  3. In a large bowl whisk together flours, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon
  4. In a medium bowl whisk together the yogurt, milk, vanilla, eggs, honey and oil
  5. Gently fold the wet mixture into the dry mixture. Using a spatula, fold until combined. Mix only until all the dry mixture disappears, fold in blueberries
  6. Divide batter evenly among prepared tin. Place pan in the oven and bake for 26 minutes, reducing temperature to 375 degrees after 9 minutes
  7. Allow muffins to cool before serving
