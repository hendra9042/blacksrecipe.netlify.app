The ingredients needed to make Miso Ramen:
  1. Take 5 hanks Fresh Chinese noodles
  2. Get 25 grams Butter
  3. Take 1 Corn on the cob
  4. Make ready 1/2 Japanese leek
  5. Prepare 1 Meat-miso
  6. Prepare 5 Seasoned Soft-Boiled Eggs
  7. Prepare 500 grams Char Siu
  8. Make ready 1 Aji Moyashi
  9. Take 2 liter Pork soup
  10. Make ready For the miso sauce:
  11. Take 150 grams Miso
  12. Take 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  13. Get 1 tbsp Mirin
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp Oyster sauce
  15. Make ready 2 tbsp Shaoxing wine

Steps to make Miso Ramen:
  1. Combine all the miso sauce ingredients and mix well. Cut the leeks into small pieces, soak in water, then drain well.
  2. Boil the noodles in a generous amount of water. While they boil, warm the bowls.
  3. Put 1.5-2 tablespoons miso sauce in each bowl, then pour in about 400 ml of the soup. Add the boiled noodles.
  4. Top with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
