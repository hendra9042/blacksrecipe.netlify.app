Steps to make Blueberry Bars:
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F
  2. In a large mixing bowl, blend cake mix, butter, and 1 egg.
  3. Pat mixture into a buttered or sprayed 15X10 jelly roll pan. You can just use a cookie pan, but be wary of spill over.
  4. Beat cream cheese until fluffy; blend in two eggs and sugar. Pour over cake mixture
  5. Spoon pie filling over top and sprinkle with coconuts and chopped nuts
  6. Bake for 45 minutes until lightly browned
  7. Cool and cut into bars.
