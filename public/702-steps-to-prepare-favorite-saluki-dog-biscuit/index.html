The ingredients needed to make Saluki Dog Biscuit:
  1. Prepare whole meal bread mix
  2. Get sugar
  3. Make ready salt
  4. Take plain white flour
  5. Get wheat germ
  6. Prepare powdered milk
  7. Take heaped active yeast
  8. Take sweet corn
  9. Take vegetable soup
  10. Make ready heaped marmite or vegemite
  11. Prepare onion
  12. Get garlic
  13. Prepare egg
  14. Prepare flax seed oil capsual
  15. Prepare butter
  16. Make ready golden syrup
Steps to make Saluki Dog Biscuit:
  1. preheat oven to 350°F or180C
  2. Mix all dry ingredients in bowl
  3. Mix all wet ingredients in another bowl.
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry.
  5. Line cookie sheet with baking paper or non stick tin foil.Put desert spoons if mixture onto sheets.Cook for an hour.Keep checking and turning biscuts.
  6. Let cool in oven they start to harden.
