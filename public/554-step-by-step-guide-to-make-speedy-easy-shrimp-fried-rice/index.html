  4. Take 3/4 cup frozen mixed vegetables

  7. Take 2 large eggs, lightly beaten

  9. Take 1/4 cup diced onion

Steps to make Easy Shrimp Fried Rice:
  1. Cook 2 cups of rice and cool while prepping the rest of ingredients.
  2. In a large non-stick skillet add the oils and heat over medium high heat. Add the shrimp and cook until nearly done, around 3 minutes. Remove shrimp with a slotted spoon and set aside.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables to the oil and cook until veggies begin to soften.
  4. Add the garlic and ginger to the veggies and cook for another minute or so.
  5. Push vegetables to one side of skillet and add eggs to s ramble, stirring as needed.
  6. Add shrimp, rice, and onion. Drizzle evenly with soy sauce and season with salt and pepper. Stir together and cook for a couple minutes until the shrimp is heated through. Serve and enjoy!
