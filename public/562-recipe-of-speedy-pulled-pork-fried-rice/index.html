Steps to make Pulled Pork Fried Rice:
  1. Heat a little oil in a wok on high heat.
  2. Add in the garlic and shallots. Saute until the garlic looks almost golden brown. Put aside on the edge of the wok.
  3. Add a little more oil in the center of the wok.
  4. Pour the beaten eggs. Scramble it.
  5. When the egg looks almost set, add the cooked rice.

  9. Add green onion. Give more stir a couple more time then turn the heat off.
  10. Serve immediately.
