The ingredients needed to make Crispy yuba rolls:
  1. Prepare 5 yuba rolls
  2. Prepare 500 cc Dashi
  3. Prepare 40 cc Soy sauce
  4. Prepare 30 cc Mirin
  5. Make ready 50 g Kadaif
  6. Prepare 1.5 cm depth in a pan Extra Virgin Olive Oil from Spain
  7. Get Tempura butter
  8. Make ready 1 cup wheat flour
  9. Take 180 cc water
  10. Make ready 1 egg

Instructions to make Crispy yuba rolls:
  1. Prepare dashi and yuba rolls in a pan and heat. When starts boiling, add soy sauce and mirin. Cook low
  2. Heat for 3 mins and turn off heat.
  3. After cools down, heat again until dashi is evaporated.
  4. Sprinkle wheat flour on yuba rolls, court with tempura butter and wrap around with kadaif.
  5. Fry the yuba rolls with Extra Virgin Olive Oil from Spain at 160 degree in Celsius.
  6. Gradually increase the temperature until the rolls become crispy.
