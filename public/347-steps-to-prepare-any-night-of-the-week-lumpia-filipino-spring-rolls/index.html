The ingredients needed to make Lumpia (Filipino Spring Rolls):
  1. Get 2.5 lbs ground pork
  2. Get 1 cup shredded carrots
  3. Take 1 cup onions
  4. Prepare 1 cup spring onions, thinly sliced
  5. Take 2 eggs
  6. Make ready 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  8. Take 2/3 cup soy sauce
  9. Make ready 2 teaspoons salt (optional)
  10. Make ready 1 Knorr pork broth cube

Instructions to make Lumpia (Filipino Spring Rolls):
  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well.
  2. Wrap about 1-1 1/2 tbsp of the mixture in a lumpia wrapper. Do this until mixture is consumed.
  3. Heat cooking oil in a frying pan and fry the lumpia until the wrapper gets golden brown.
  4. Put the fried lumpia in a serving plate and let excess oil drip.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
