The ingredients needed to make Stir fried cucumber with eggs (chicken or pork):
  1. Prepare 1 largh cucumber
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 1 clove garlic
  4. Prepare 4 tbsp soya sauce (I used Macci)
  5. Prepare 100 grams pork or chicken (an option)
  6. Make ready 3 tbsp vegetable oil or sunflower oil
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup water
