The ingredients needed to make Cone Sandwich:
  1. Get 1 cup chicken boiled
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp mayonnaise
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp yogurt
  4. Get 2 tbsp pickled chilli chopped
  5. Prepare 2 egg boiled & chopped
  6. Prepare 1 tsp hot sauce
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup salad leaves
  10. Take 1 tbsp black olive
  11. Prepare Bread slice as required
