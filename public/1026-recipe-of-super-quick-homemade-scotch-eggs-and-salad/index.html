The ingredients needed to make Scotch Eggs and Salad:
  1. Make ready 6 hard-boiled eggs, well chilled
  2. Get 6 sausages. Remove outer casing
  3. Prepare 1 ⁄2 cup flour
  4. Get 3 eggs, beaten
  5. Make ready 1 cup breadcrumbs
  6. Get Vegetable oil for deepfrying

Steps to make Scotch Eggs and Salad:
  1. Unshell eggs. - Divide sausage into 6 equal portions.
  2. Roll each egg in flour then wrap the sausage meat around each egg.
  3. Dip sausage-wrapped eggs into beaten egg and roll in bread crumbs.
  4. Roll in egg once more.
  5. In heated oil, cook each egg for 4-5 minutes or until browned all round and cooked.
  6. Drain on paper towels, Let cool. - Serve with your favourite Salad.
