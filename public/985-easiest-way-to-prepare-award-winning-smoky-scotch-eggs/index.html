The ingredients needed to make Smoky scotch eggs:
  1. Take 450 g sausagemeat
  2. Take 5 free range eggs
  3. Take 1/2 (1 bunch) chives
  4. Take 1/2 of bunch flat leaf parsley
  5. Take 1 packet panko breadcrumbs
  6. Take 1 couple of squirts of sriracha
  7. Make ready 1 tsp smoked paprika
  8. Prepare 2 tsp English mustard
  9. Take 1/4 of a tsp of grated nutmeg
  10. Prepare Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Smoky scotch eggs:
  1. Boil 3 of the eggs for 3-4mins so they are soft boiled. Cool and peel. Tip - use older eggs to aid peeling.
  2. Mix the sausagemeat, mustard, paprika, sriracha, nutmeg, chives (chopped), parsley (chopped), salt and pepper for seasoning.
  3. Make into 3 patties, wrap around the egg gently until sealed. Put flour on your hands to make it easier to roll.
  4. Beat 2 eggs and add to 1 plate, add flour and panko to 2 other plates. Roll the balls in flour, then eggs and finally panko breadcrumbs.
  5. Heat oil in a the deep fat fryer to 150c. Gently add each ball for 4-5mins until golden.
  6. Drain and put on a plate with kitchen roll to remove excess oil.
  7. Serve with picallili. Enjoy!
