The ingredients needed to make Peppermint Puffs:
  1. Take 2/3 cup shortening
  2. Take 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  4. Take 1 egg or equivalent of egg substitute
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 cups flour of choice (if gluten-free will need to add xanthum gum, if your flour doesn't have it already added)
  6. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup crushed Peppermint candy or candy canes
Steps to make Peppermint Puffs:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease baking sheet.
  2. Cream together shortening, granulated sugar, & brown sugar. Beat in egg. Add flour, baking powder, & salt. Blend well. Stir in crushed Peppermint.
  3. Shape dough into 1 inch balls & place on cookie sheets. Bake for 15 minutes. Remove & cool.
