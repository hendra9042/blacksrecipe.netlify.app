The ingredients needed to make Lauren's Deviled Eggs:
  1. Take 6-12 eggs
  2. Get 1/2 tsp Louisiana Hot Sauce
  3. Take 2 tbsp yellow mustard
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup mayo
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp granulated white sugar
  6. Get 1/8 tsp salt
  7. Take 1/2 tsp onion powder
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp pickle juice
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp butter (room temperature)
  11. Make ready 5 bacon slices (crispy)
  12. Get Pickled Mustard Seeds (see recipe)
  13. Prepare Fresh or dried thyme
  14. Make ready Dried dill weed

Steps to make Lauren's Deviled Eggs:
  1. Boil the eggs.
  2. Fry your bacon.
  3. Drain hot water from eggs and add cold water… for an easy peel.
  4. Add wet ingredients to a small bowl. Stir till well combined.
  5. Add dry ingredients in small bowl. Stir till well combined.
  6. Peel eggs and slice horizontally.
  7. Add all the yolks to a bowl, along with all the ingredients. Make sure your yolks have broken up really good or use a food processor or hand mixer to mix up yolks.
  8. Pipe your yolks onto eggs or use spoon. Top with piece of bacon and pickled Mustard seeds and garnish with thyme and or dill weed
