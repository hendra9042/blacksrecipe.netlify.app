Steps to make Scotched eggs:

  3. Roll the uncoated eggs into the thin sausage meat to achieve a perfect coat on the outside
  4. Beat some eggs on a different plate. Deep the coated eggs into the beat egg and then to the bread crumbs to form a coating.
