Instructions to make Poached eggs and avocado on muffins:
  1. Boil water and place in a pan on medium to high heat.
  2. Crack 1 egg into cup and carefully lower into water. Repeat with 2nd egg.
  3. Cut muffin in half and toast in toaster. Cover each half with mashed avocado and put salt & pepper on it.
  4. When eggs are poached as you like them (I usually go for about 4 minutes so middle is really runny still) remove with slotted spoon, making sure all water is drained and place one egg on each half.
