Steps to make Poached Egg:
  1. Boil your water….it should be extremely hot
  2. Add in a tablespoon or two or your vinegar and a pinch of salt
  3. Break your egg into the boiling water. Take care not to burn and do not stir
  4. Turn down the heat and cover your cooking pot. Let the egg till the yolks turn color and stop being runny….
  5. Once ready, gently remove them from the water.
