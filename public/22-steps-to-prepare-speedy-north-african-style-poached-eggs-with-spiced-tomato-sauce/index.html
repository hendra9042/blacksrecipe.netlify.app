Instructions to make North African-Style Poached Eggs with Spiced Tomato Sauce:
  1. Roughly chop garlic, onions, and bell peppers and tomatoes.
  2. Hot pan, olive oil. Sweat out the veggies until soft. Season with salt and pepper then put all the spices in.
  3. With your spoon, make small pockets in the pan then crack your eggs in.
  4. Lightly season the eggs then transfer into a pre-heated oven 350F for 5-10mins, depending on how you like your eggs.
  5. Top with chopped spring onions. Serve over sourdough bread and hot coffee. Bon appetit.
