  5. Prepare 1/2 cup Warm, not hot water

Instructions to make Country House Rolls:
  1. Scald milk
  2. Add sugar and salt to milk and then cool to Lukewarm
  3. Dissolve yeast in warm, not hot water; add to lukewarm milk mixture
  4. Add 3 cups flour and stir until smooth
  5. Add eggs and softened margarine or butter; beat until smooth
  6. Gradually add remaining flour mixing well after each addition.
  7. Turn dough out onto lightly floured board
  8. Knead until smooth and elastic, approx 10min
  9. Place in greased bowl.
  10. Cover and let rise in warm place, free from draft, until doubled in bulk. About 11/2 to 2 hours
  11. Punch down
  12. Let rise again until doubled, about another 35-45min
  13. Punch down and turn out onto board
  14. Cover dough and let rest 15min
  15. Roll dough 1/4in thick. Cut with biscuit cutter
  16. Cover and let rise in warm place for 15-20 min
  17. Brush with melted margarine or butter
  18. Place close together on greased pan.
  19. Bake 425°F 12-15min
  20. Remove from oven and brush with margarine or butter
