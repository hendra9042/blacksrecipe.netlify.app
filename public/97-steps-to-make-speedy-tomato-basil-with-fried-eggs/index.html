Instructions to make Tomato Basil with Fried Eggs:
  1. In a frying pan under medium heat, add 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil and bring to a light bubble. Add in garlic and lightly stir for about thirty seconds.
  2. Add in shallots, and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Next add in the Brussel sprout and continue to saut√© until a light char crisp develops. Add in seasoning salt and mix well.
  4. Add in soup and stir. Bring to a low heat and let the flavors meld together. Add salt and pepper to season and stir.
  5. For the eggs, fry them to your liking.
  6. Add soup in a bowl, and top with 2 eggs each. Top with ground pepper and green onion.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
