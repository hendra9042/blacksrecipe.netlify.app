Instructions to make Teriyaki Chikuwa Rolls with Quail Eggs:
  1. Boil the quail eggs and peels the shells. Cut the chikuwa in half lengthwise. Wrap around the quail eggs, and secure with toothpicks.
  2. Put the katakuriko and Step 1 into a plastic bag as shown in the picture, and gently shake to coat.
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan, and fry Step 2 until golden brown. When they become crispy, wipe off the excess oil with a paper towel.
  4. Add all the teriyaki sauce ingredients to Step 3, and when they become shiny, they are done.
