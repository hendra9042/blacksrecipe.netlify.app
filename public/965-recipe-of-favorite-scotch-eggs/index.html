The ingredients needed to make Scotch Eggs:
  1. Make ready 6 eggs
  2. Take 1 lb ground pork
  3. Get 1 tbsp Worchestershire
  4. Take 1 tbsp English mustard
  5. Prepare 2 tsp cornstarch
  6. Take 1/4 tsp mace
  7. Take 2 leaves sage, chopped
  8. Make ready 2 sprigs thyme, chopped
  9. Prepare 1 salt
  10. Take 1 pepper
  11. Take 1/4 cup milk
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup flour
  13. Prepare 2 cup panko crumbs

Instructions to make Scotch Eggs:
  1. Place 6 eggs in a 2qt saucepan and cover by 1" with cold water. Place over high heat and bring to boil; cover, remove from heat, let sit for 6 minutes. Drain eggs, and transfer to a bowl of ice water; let sit for 5 minutes. Drain eggs, and peel and discard shells, set aside.
  2. Combine sausage, Worcestershire, mustard, cornstarch, mace, sage and thyme in a bowl; season with salt and pepper and mix until evenly combined. Divide mixture into 6 equal portions, and form portion around each cooked egg to cover completely. Place on a plate and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
