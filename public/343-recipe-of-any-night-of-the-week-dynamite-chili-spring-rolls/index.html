The ingredients needed to make Dynamite (Chili Spring Rolls):
  1. Make ready for the filling:
  2. Make ready 1/4 kg ground beef
  3. Take 1/4 kg ground pork
  4. Get 6 Chorizo (finely chopped)
  5. Prepare 1 medium carrot (grated)
  6. Make ready 2 large onions (finely choped)
  7. Get 5 clover of garlic (finely choped)
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup cheddar cheese (grated)
  9. Take 2 tbsp onion powder
  10. Get 2 tsp paprika
  11. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  13. Make ready 2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional)
  14. Get 2 tbsp oregano
  15. Get 3 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  16. Make ready 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  17. Take 2 medium eggs
  18. Make ready for wrapping the dynamite
  19. Get Spring roll wrapper
  20. Prepare 2 tsp cornstarch dissolved in water
  21. Prepare Large or medium green chili

Instructions to make Dynamite (Chili Spring Rolls):
  1. For the filling, mix all the ingredients and cook it to medium heat for 15 to 20 mins.
  2. Let it cool after cooking the filling while removing the seeds of the chili.
  3. Add the filling to the chili that has no seeds. And wrap it using spring roll wrapper. Use cornstarch water mixture to seal the wrapper.
  4. Deep fry the dynamite using canola oil. Serve and eat.
