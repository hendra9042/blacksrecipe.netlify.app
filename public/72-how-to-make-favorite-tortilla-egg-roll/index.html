The ingredients needed to make Tortilla Egg Roll:
  1. Get 1 Tortilla
  2. Make ready 2 Eggs
  3. Make ready 1 green capsicum in sliced
  4. Get 1 onion in sliced
  5. Make ready 1 carrot in sliced
  6. Prepare to taste Salt
  7. Get 2 tsp red chilli flakes
  8. Take 1 tbs Ranch/Mayonnaise
  9. Make ready 1 tsp Tomato ketchup
  10. Take 2 tsp olive oil
  11. Make ready 1 tsp schezwan sauce
Steps to make Tortilla Egg Roll:
  1. For filling..Take a pan add 1 tsp oil. Add sliced onion, carrot & capsicum.
  2. Add salt, 1 tsp schezwan sauce, salt to taste mix well & saute for 1 min.
  3. Then take them out in a bowl.
  4. Now in a small cup add 2 eggs, salt & red chilli flakes..beat well.
  5. Now heat up same pan again with 1 tsp of olive oil.Add the egg batter after 30 sec.place the Tortilla upon that..press gently.
  6. Now flip it over & cook for 30 sec.on medium heat.
  7. Then take it out in a plate.Add ranch & tomato ketchup..spread all over that.
  8. Now put the vegetable filling upon that. Roll it tightly & cut into half as per your choice
  9. Serve hot with ketchup & some crunchy vegetables and enjoy.
