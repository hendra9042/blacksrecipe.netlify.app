Steps to make Baked egg:
  1. Preheat oven to 425. Spray muffin tin with cooking oil
  2. In a muffin tin break 1 egg in each hole (can cook 1-12 at a time)
  3. Pour heavy cream on top of the egg and don’t mix then add salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Top with shredded cheese and bake 12-13 minutes
