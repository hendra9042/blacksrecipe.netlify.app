Instructions to make Poached Eggs:
  1. First take a vessel add 250ml water and bring that to a boil.
  2. Add salt and vinegar to the water
  3. Now carefully crack an egg in a small bowl.Be careful and don't let the yolk deform.
  4. Now very carefully add the egg to the boiling water.Close the water and let that poach for 2-3mins on a low flame. Don't cook it for long or else yolk will not be runny.
  5. Now using a spoon remove that poached egg from water. Seasone it with some salt, black pepper, oregano and coriander leaves
