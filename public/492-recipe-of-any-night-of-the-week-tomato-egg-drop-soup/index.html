The ingredients needed to make Tomato egg drop soup:
  1. Prepare 2 egg
  2. Get 3 tomato
  3. Get 3 teaspoons black pepper powder
  4. Get 2 teaspoon ginger
  5. Get 1 teaspoon black pepper
  6. Take 1 bayleaf
  7. Prepare 1 butter
  8. Take 1 teaspoon coriander leaves
  9. Prepare As needed salt

Steps to make Tomato egg drop soup:
  1. First cut tomato coriander keep aside
  2. Add butter in the pan
  3. Add butter in the pan add black pepper add crushed ginger fry lightly add tomatoes stir fry
  4. At that time take separate bowl broke egg one by one add salt add black pepper powder keep stirring
  5. Pour egg in a slow way in the tomato gravy Add salt and black pepper powder cover with the lid after five minutes of slow cooking remove from the oven add to the soup bowl spread coriander leaves
  6. Delicious soup is ready
