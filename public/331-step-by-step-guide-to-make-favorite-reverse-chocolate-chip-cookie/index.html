Steps to make Reverse chocolate chip cookie:
  1. Beat butter and sugar together
  2. Add egg and vanilla extract
  3. Melt semi sweet chocolate and add it
  4. Mix dry ingredients together then add them to the dough and mix, make sure not to over mix
  5. Fold in sour cream or yoghurt, and the white chocolate chips
  6. Chill the dough for at least half an hour before scooping it on the baking sheet
  7. Bake at 350⁰F or 175⁰C for 10-12 mins
