The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Paste Fried Rice:
  1. Make ready Cooking oil
  2. Take 1/2 onion, diced
  3. Take 3 cups rice, cooked
  4. Prepare 1 clove garlic, minced
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp shrimp paste or substitute
  6. Prepare Salt to taste
  7. Prepare 1/2 oz cooking wine
  8. Prepare 3/4 cup soy meat, diced
  9. Take 2 large eggs, scrambled
  10. Get 1 tsp brown sugar
  11. Get 2 tsp lemon or lime juice
  12. Make ready 1.5 cups lettuce, shredded
