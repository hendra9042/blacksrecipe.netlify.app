The ingredients needed to make Simple Absolutely Crumbly Egg Fried Rice:
  1. Make ready 200 grams Hot cooked white rice
  2. Get 1 Egg
  3. Take 2 slice Bacon
  4. Prepare 2 stick Asparagus
  5. Prepare 1 dash Salt and pepper
  6. Get 1 dash Soy sauce
  7. Get 1 tbsp Vegetable oil

Instructions to make Simple Absolutely Crumbly Egg Fried Rice:
  1. Cut the bacon into 1 cm wide strips. Peel the root ends of the asparagus and cut into 1 cm long pieces.
  2. Beat the egg and add salt and pepper. Then mix into the rice.
  3. Heat a frying pan and add the bacon and asparagus. Once they've cooked, remove and set aside.
  4. In the same frying pan, heat the vegetable oil, add the rice from Step 2 and cook.
  5. Once the rice has become crumbly, add the ingredients from Step 3 and combine. Drizzle a bit of soy sauce and mix together briskly.
