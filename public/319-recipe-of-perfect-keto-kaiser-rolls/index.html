The ingredients needed to make KETO Kaiser Rolls:
  1. Prepare 7 eggs
  2. Prepare 1 stick melted real butter
  3. Prepare 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (reserve 1/3 cup for topping)
  4. Make ready 2 cups almond flour
  5. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Take 1/2 tsp xantham gum
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp onion powder
  9. Get 12 tsp parsley flakes (topper)

Steps to make KETO Kaiser Rolls:
  1. Melt butter, add eggs to large bowl and whip on high for 2 to 3 minutes. Add butter, blend again. Add dry ingredients directly to wet batter bowl, excluding parsley flakes, and blend on low till very thick. Add 2/3 cup of cheddar cheese and blend into batter.
  2. Line Cookie sheet with parchment paper. Use 1/2 cup of batter per roll and dollop onto tray, forming your rolls shape. Sprinkle with shredded cheese and parsley flakes.
  3. Bake in preheated 350°oven till done approx 18 to 20 minutes.
