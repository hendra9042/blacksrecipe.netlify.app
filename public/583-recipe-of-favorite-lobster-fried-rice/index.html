The ingredients needed to make Lobster Fried Rice:
  1. Take 2 Cooked Lobsters
  2. Get 2 cups cooked white rice, short grain or long grain
  3. Get 2 Green onions sliced
  4. Prepare 1 Tbsp Chopped garlic
  5. Prepare 1 tsp sea salt or kosher salt
  6. Take 1 tsp red pepper flakes
  7. Prepare 2 eggs scrambled
  8. Prepare 2 Tbsp Vegetable oil
  9. Take 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  10. Make ready 1/4 chicken broth

Instructions to make Lobster Fried Rice:
  1. Take lobster meat out of its shell and chop up into cubes not too small, set aside
  2. In a large sauce pan or wok add the oil once heated up add green onion and garlic,salt and pepper saute for about 1 to 2 mins don't burn garlic
  3. Add rice saute add soy sauce mix thoroughly then add lobster mix to combine then add scrambled egg cook for 2 mins if you need more liquid add 1/4 cup chicken broth
  4. Place on serving plate enjoy..
