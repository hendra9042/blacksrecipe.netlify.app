The ingredients needed to make Egg Fried Rice:
  1. Get 2 eggs
  2. Get 4 cups cooked short grain rice
  3. Prepare 1 tsp salt

Instructions to make Egg Fried Rice:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat eggs and salt. Add cold rice into the mixing bowl and coat rice well with egg mixture
  2. Heat a wok and add oil. Add the rice into the wok and keep stirring. Cook until all the egg and rice is cooked through. Season with pepper and more salt if necessary. Serve hot.
