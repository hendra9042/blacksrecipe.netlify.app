The ingredients needed to make Bacon Kimchi Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 plates cooked white rice (leftover rice, put in the refrigerator for the night, is recommended)
  2. Make ready 8 pcs bacon, cut into small pieces
  3. Make ready 3 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Make ready 150 gr kimchi, cut into small pieces
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp soy sauce
  6. Take 1/4 tsp salt or to taste
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp ground white pepper or to taste
  8. Take 3 eggs
  9. Take 1/2 tablespoon vegetable oil for frying eggs

Kimchi fried rice (Kimchi Bokkeumbap, 김치 볶음밥) is a type of Korean fried rice and it is predominantly cooked with kimchi (obviously!) Instructions to make Bacon Kimchi Fried Rice:
  1. In a skillet, fry the bacon. Allow it to render and become crisp. Take it out of the wok and set aside. Discard the oil. Reserving 1 tbsp in the pan.
  2. Saute garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the kimchi and it’s water.
  4. Add the fried bacon. Stir well.
  5. Add the white rice.
  6. Season with soy sauce, salt, and ground pepper. Stir well. Adjust to taste. - Remove from the heat. Place it on serving plates.
  7. Fry the eggs according to your taste. Remove from the heat. Place them on top of fried rice. Serve immediately. Yum! 😋
