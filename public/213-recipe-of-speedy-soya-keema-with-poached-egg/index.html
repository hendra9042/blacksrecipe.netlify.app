Instructions to make Soya keema with poached egg:
  1. Steam the soya keema with salt
  2. Make paste of coriander leaves and ginger garlic
  3. Marinate the granules with the masala paste and curd
  4. Heat a pan and fry the onions till it becomes golden
  5. Add the marinated granules and mix well adding red chilli powder
  6. Add tomato sauce and mix well
  7. Add garam masala powder and switch off the heat
  8. Now plate it on a microwave safe dish
  9. Take the egg and broke it gently
  10. Spread it over the cooked keema and keep it on high micro for 3-4 mnts
  11. Soya keema with poached egg is ready to be served with any type of flat bread or paratha
