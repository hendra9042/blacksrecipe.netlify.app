Steps to make Quick Coffee Cake with Raw Fruit Adapted by Rebecca Sackheim:
  1. Sift together the flour, salt, baking powder and sugar
  2. Add egg, milk and butter.
  3. Mix all to a soft dough, beat well.
  4. Add fruit
  5. Turn into a greased pan, ; spread evenly
  6. Bake in a moderate oven (350) for about 25 minutes.
  7. Brush over with melted butter and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
