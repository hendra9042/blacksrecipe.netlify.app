The ingredients needed to make Poached Eggs:
  1. Take Poached Egg
  2. Make ready 2 Eggs
  3. Take 1 tsp Salt
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp Vinegar
  5. Prepare Serving Suggestion
  6. Make ready 2 English Muffins
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup Smoked Fish
  8. Take 2 tsp Butter
  9. Prepare 1 Avocado
  10. Prepare 1 Parsley for decoration

How to poach eggs: Gently boil water in a large pot. If you're thinking, "What the heck is a gentle boil?" just bring the water to a boil and then lower the heat, so the water is just bubbling but not boiling like crazy. Fresh eggs work best for poaching. Their whites hold together better than older eggs.

Steps to make Poached Eggs:
  1. Pour three to four inches of water into a large pot. Bring water to simmer.
  2. Add the salt and the vinegar to the water
  3. Crack one egg into a recipient, and put it carefully into the simmering water.
  4. Using a slotted spoon keep bringing the eggs whites together. It will look messy, but no worries, it will be ok :)
  5. Cook it for 1 minute, and then use the slotted spoon to remove the eggs from the water.
  6. Serving suggestion: Toast 2 english muffins, spread butter, add the avocado and the smoked fish.
  7. Put the egg on top of the smoked fish, then add one slice of avocado and some fresh parsley to give it some color :D
