The ingredients needed to make Boiled noodles with egg poach:
  1. Prepare 100 gm noodles
  2. Prepare some Beans, chopped
  3. Prepare Some Carrots, chopped
  4. Get some Capsicum, chopped
  5. Get some spring onions
  6. Make ready 1 egg
  7. Take 1/2 tsp black pepper
  8. Get 1 tsp salt
  9. Take 1/2 tsp oil or butter
  10. Get 1 tsp tomato sauce

Instructions to make Boiled noodles with egg poach:

  3. Then take out the noodles & vegetables..
