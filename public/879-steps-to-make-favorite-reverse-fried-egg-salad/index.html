The ingredients needed to make Reverse fried egg salad:
  1. Get eggs
  2. Get 6 large eggs
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Get 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp water
  6. Take 1/2 stick margarine
  7. Get dressing
  8. Get 1 tsp mayonnaise
  9. Make ready 1 tsp yellow prepared mustard
  10. Prepare 1 tsp Heinz ketchup
  11. Prepare 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
  12. Take 2 tsp grated parmesan cheese

Instructions to make Reverse fried egg salad:
  1. With a pan and a tight fitting lid, heat the margarine.
  2. Crack the eggs add salt and pepper. Beat the eggs well add to the pan. Cover let cook rolling pan occasionally.

  5. When the eggs are done roll eggs into a roll use gravity to help roll. By tilting pan as you roll.
