  1. Make ready 1/4 Cup Quick Grits
  2. Take 1 Cup Water
  3. Take 2 tbsp Milk
  4. Take Salt to taste
  5. Prepare 1 slice Colby jack cheese
  6. Prepare Protein
  7. Make ready 1 Large Egg
  8. Take 1 oz Fresh Spinach

Instructions to make Grits bowl W/ Fried egg & Spinach:
  1. Bring Water to a Boil, with a Pinch of Salt.
  2. Add Grits to boiling Water. Stir Consistently with fork or Whisk for 2 minutes. Do not walk away from boiling grits as they will dry and stick to pot.
  3. Reduce heat to Low Simmer and cover with Lid for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Stir/Whisk Grits vigorously, If needed add a bit more water. Otherwise add 2tbsp of Milk at this time. Add half Slice of cheese. Mix well. Cover with Lid. Remove from heat.
  5. Grease Skillet and Fry Egg on Medium-high Heat. Season to taste.
  6. Once Egg is cooked, Wilt Spinach in Skillet.
  7. Spoon Grits into a Bowl - Place spinach over grits, top with Egg and Cheese. Enjoy !
