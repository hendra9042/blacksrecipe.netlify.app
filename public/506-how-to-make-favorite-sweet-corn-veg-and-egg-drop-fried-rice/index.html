The ingredients needed to make Sweet corn veg and egg drop fried rice:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 cup cooked rice
  2. Get 3 tbsp sweet corn
  3. Get 1 tbsp beans chopped finely
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp carrot chopped finely
  5. Prepare 1 capsicum chopped finely
  6. Make ready 1 onion chopped finely
  7. Prepare 1 tsp finely chopped garlic

  14. Take 2 eggs
