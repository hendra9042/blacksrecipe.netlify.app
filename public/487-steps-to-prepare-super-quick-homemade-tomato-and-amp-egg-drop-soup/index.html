The ingredients needed to make Tomato & Egg-drop Soup:
  1. Prepare 1/4 Onion
  2. Take 8 Petit tomatoes
  3. Take 1 tablespoon Sesame oil
  4. Take 2 cup Water
  5. Take 1 teaspoon Vinegar
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon Chicken soup stock
  7. Take 2 Eggs

Instructions to make Tomato & Egg-drop Soup:
  1. Finely chop 1/4 of an onion. Chop small tomatoes in small pieces. Crack eggs into a bowl.
  2. Heat sesame oil in a pan and stir-fry onion.
  3. Pour 2 cups of water, add chicken soup stock and vinegar. Add tomatoes. Bring the soup to boil and slowly pour beaten eggs. Finish!
