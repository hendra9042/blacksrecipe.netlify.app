The ingredients needed to make Korean Ramen/Ramyeon:

  2. Get 1 egg
  3. Take 1 spring onion
  4. Get 1 chinese cabbage or whatever veg u have
  5. Get 2 tbsp tomato sauce (del monte tomato sauce is highly recommended)
  6. Get 2 sausages or fish or meat (optional)
  7. Make ready 1 pinch black pepper (optional)
  8. Take Seasoning (i used instant noodle seasoning)
  9. Get Water

Steps to make Korean Ramen/Ramyeon:
  1. I actually dont like raw veg so i boiled cabbage n spring onion for about 2 minutes, here. Then i chopped them
  2. Boil instant noodle, tomato souce n cracked egg in same pot. Boil them for 5 minutes
  3. Chop sausages or meat
  4. After step 2 is ready, put them in a bowl n mix it with seasoning n also black pepper.
  5. Then give garnish (chopped cabbage, sausage, chopped spring onion or whatever you like but korean ramen mostly use chopped spring onion)
  6. Korean ramyung is ready to serve :)))
