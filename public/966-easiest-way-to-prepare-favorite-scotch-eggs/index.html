The ingredients needed to make Scotch Eggs:
  1. Get 6 hard-cooked eggs, well chilled
  2. Get 1 lb bulk sausage meat
  3. Take 2 tbsp finely chopped parsley
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp ground sage
  5. Get 1/4 tsp pepper
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup flour
  7. Prepare 2 eggs, beaten
  8. Get 1/2 to 3/4 up bread crumbs
  9. Take 1 vegetable oil for trying

Instructions to make Scotch Eggs:
  1. This traditional British picnic favorite is simple to prepare. Make sure that eggs are well chilled … sausage will wrap around them more easily.
  2. Combine sausage, parsley, sage and pepper in a large mixing bowl, mixing well. Divide meat mixture into six equal portions.
  3. Press meat mixture around eggs with hands, keeping the oval shape.
  4. Sprinkle eggs with flour, coating lightly on all sides. Dip into beaten egg and the roll in bread crumbs.
  5. Heat vegetable oil in deep fryer or deep heavy saucepan to 350°F on deep-fat thermometer. Cook one egg at a time for about 4 to 5 minutes or until well browned.
  6. Drain on paper toweling; cool; refrigerate.
  7. To serve, slice into thick slices and fan out on plate.
