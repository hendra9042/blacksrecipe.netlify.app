The ingredients needed to make Egg mimosa salad with vegetables, tuna👍😝😝:
  1. Take 50 g green beans cut into small cubes,steamed
  2. Make ready 1 tomato cut into small cubes
  3. Take 2 tablespoon mayonnaise
  4. Prepare 2 tablespoon cottage cheese
  5. Make ready steamed diced carot
  6. Make ready vinaigrette ; tablespoon of lemon juice
  7. Prepare tablespoon orange juice
  8. Make ready 3 tablespoon oil olive
  9. Prepare pinch salt and black pepper and thyme and garlic powder
  10. Get a few lettuce leaves
  11. Prepare 50 g green olives
  12. Make ready 20 g steamed rice
  13. Take 1 can tuna
  14. Get 2 hard boiled eggs finely diced egg whites
  15. Get grated eggs yolk
  16. Take parsley for garnish

Steps to make Egg mimosa salad with vegetables, tuna👍😝😝:
  1. Mix the cut and cooked vegetables with lemon and orange juice and a pinch of salt and pepper zest and a pinch of thyme and add the cooked rice then add the mayonnaise and cottage cheese mix well and let cool.
  2. Mix the cut egg whites and mayonnaise and let cool. mix the chopped tomato with salt, lemon juice and olive oil and set aside.
  3. Wash the lettuce and put the leaves on a flat plate and put a round cookie cutter in the middle of the plate and put the first layer of vegetables, rice with mayonnaise and cheese then the second layer of diced tomatoes then the chopped egg whites with mayonnaise then the third layer the tuna season with salt and pepper and the juice of a lemon and zest then the last layer the grated egg yolks.
  4. Garnish them with the green olives, diced tomatoes and parsley.
