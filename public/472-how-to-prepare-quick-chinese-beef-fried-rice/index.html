Steps to make Chinese Beef Fried Rice:
  1. Heat the sesame oil
  2. Add minced garlic. Stir for 5-10 seconds
  3. Crack the egg. Add into the pan. Beat it on the pan.
  4. Add sliced beef. Stir untill the beef cooked well and soft.
  5. Add the bok choy. Wait for 30-40 seconds
  6. Add the rice. Stir for 30 seconds.
  7. Add the salt soy sauce. Stir until it mixed well.
  8. Add the salt. Stir again until it mixed well.
