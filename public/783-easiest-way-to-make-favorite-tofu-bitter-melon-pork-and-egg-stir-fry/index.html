The ingredients needed to make Tofu, Bitter melon, Pork and Egg Stir fry:
  1. Take 1 package Tofu
  2. Get 300 g bitter melon
  3. Prepare 150-200 g thinly sliced polk
  4. Make ready 150 g bean sprouts
  5. Prepare 3 eggs
  6. Take ☆2 TBSP oyster sauce
  7. Get ☆2 TBSP soy sauce
  8. Prepare ☆1.5 TBSP sake
  9. Make ready ☆1.5 tsp hondashi
  10. Get 2 TBSP sesame oil

Steps to make Tofu, Bitter melon, Pork and Egg Stir fry:
  1. Cut bitter melon.
  2. In a small bowl, give bitter melon massage to remove bitterness with a dash of salt, 1 minute. Add hot water and leave it for 10 to 30 minutes.
  3. Wrap tofu with paper towels and microwave for 2 minutes to remove water.
  4. Cut pork into 2 inch lengths. Sprinkle with salt, pepper and flour to give a base taste.
  5. In a large pan, high heat, pour oil and sauté tofu until golden brown.

  7. In the same pan, sauté pork, bitter melon and washed bean sprouts, 5 - 7 minutes. Add ☆sauce until the sauce is well mixed.
  8. Add beaten egg in the same pan with other ingredients and scramble the egg, 1 minute. Turn off the heat, Pour sesame oil at the end.
