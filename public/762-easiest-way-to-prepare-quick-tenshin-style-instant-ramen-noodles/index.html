Instructions to make Tenshin-Style Instant Ramen Noodles:
  1. Cut the imitation crab sticks into 1 cm pieces. Thinly chop the green onion diagonally.
  2. Put all the Chinese omelet ingredients in a bowl, mix, then fry in a frying pan to make a Chinese omelet.
  3. Cook the instant ramen noodles following the instructions on the package. Put the Chinese omelet on top, and it's done!
