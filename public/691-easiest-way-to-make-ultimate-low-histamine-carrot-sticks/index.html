Instructions to make Low histamine carrot sticks:
  1. Combine flours and baking powder and whisk in the bowl
  2. Grate the carrots really fine
  3. Add the carrots, softened butter and salt into the bowl and knead the dough
  4. Roll dough into a thin square and cut out sticks. I used the cooking cutter
  5. Transfer the sticks to a baking sheet, brush wisked yolks on the sticks and sprinkle with seeds and salt
  6. Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
