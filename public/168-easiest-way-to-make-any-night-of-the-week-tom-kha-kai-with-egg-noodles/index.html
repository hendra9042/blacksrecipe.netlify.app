The ingredients needed to make Tom kha kai with egg noodles:
  1. Make ready 200 g egg noodles
  2. Make ready 400 g chicken
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp. tom kha spices
  4. Prepare 400 ml coconut milk
  5. Take 300 g frozen wok vegetables
  6. Make ready Oil
  7. Prepare Fresh coriander (optional)

Steps to make Tom kha kai with egg noodles:
  1. Cook the egg noodles, then rinse them under cold water.
  2. Chop the chicken up into small cubes, and fry them in some oil.
  3. In a large pot, pour in the coconut milk, 400ml of water, and the spices. Heat up to a boil.
  4. Add the chicken and the vegetables, and cook it for 5 minutes.
  5. Serve in a bowl: add some of the egg noodles, pour on the soup, and add fresh chopped coriander on top.
