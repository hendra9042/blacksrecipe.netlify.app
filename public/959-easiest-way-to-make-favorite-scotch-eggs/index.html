Steps to make Scotch Eggs:
  1. cook and shell eggs
  2. preheat oven to 425
  3. when eggs are cool enough to handle , wrap each egg in breakfast sausage and place on baking sheet
  4. bake until sausage is cooked- about 10-15 minutes
  5. cool completely
  6. when cooled, wrap each in one triangle of crescent dough, stretching over the entire egg/sausage combo
  7. reduce heat according to crescent roll directions. place on baking sheet and bake until browned- about 10 minutes
