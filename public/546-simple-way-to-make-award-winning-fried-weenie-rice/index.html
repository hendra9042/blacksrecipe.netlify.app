The ingredients needed to make Fried Weenie Rice:
  1. Prepare 1 tbsp butter
  2. Take 1 onion chopped to liking
  3. Make ready 12 baby carrots (about 3 medium carrots) chopped
  4. Take 4 hotdogs coined and halved
  5. Get 2 garlic cloves
  6. Get 1 tbsp sesame oil
  7. Get 1 1/2 cup cooked rice
  8. Take 2 eggs lightly beaten
  9. Get 1 cup frozen peas
  10. Get 2 tbsp soy sauce
  11. Make ready 1 scallion sliced thinly

Instructions to make Fried Weenie Rice:
  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt butter.
  2. Add onions, carrots, and hot dogs and cook until onions become translucent, 3-5 minutes. Stir occasionally
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Add sesame oil to the pan and let heat 1 minute add the rice, and let cook for 3 minutes. Stir occasionally
  5. Add rice and let cook for 3 minutes, stir occasionally
  6. Push rice to one side of the pan and add the eggs, scrambling them in the pan and incorporating them into the rice when they are fully cooked.
  7. Add the peas and the soy sauce and stir to combine
  8. Top with scallions if desired and serve.
