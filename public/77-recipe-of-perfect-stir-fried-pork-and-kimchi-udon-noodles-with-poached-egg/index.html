The ingredients needed to make Stir-fried Pork and Kimchi Udon Noodles with Poached Egg:
  1. Make ready 2 portions Frozen udon noodles
  2. Take 150 grams Thinly sliced pork
  3. Make ready 150 grams Kimchi
  4. Take 6 Green onions
  5. Prepare 2 Soft-poached eggs
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp Soy sauce
  7. Get 1 tbsp Mayonnaise
  8. Make ready 1 Salt (to taste)
  9. Get 1 Pepper (to taste)
  10. Get 1 Sesame oil (to taste)

Instructions to make Stir-fried Pork and Kimchi Udon Noodles with Poached Egg:
  1. Cook the frozen udon noodles. Cut the pork and kimchi into bite sizes. Thinly lice the green onion diagonally.
  2. Heat sesame oil in a frying pan and put the pork. Season with salt and pepper and stir-fry until cooked through. Add the kimchi with its pickling brine and stir-fry.
  3. Add the udon noodles and mix roughly. Add the soy sauce and mayonnaise and fry. Add the green onion and cook quickly. Transfer to a serving dish. Place a poached egg on top and garnish with sesame seeds.
