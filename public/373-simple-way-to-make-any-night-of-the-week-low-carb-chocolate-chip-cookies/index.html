Steps to make Low Carb Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Pre heat oven to 350°F
  2. In a medium-sized bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a small saucepan over medium-low heat, belt the butter, stirring constantly until it reaches a golden brown color; remove from heat immediately.
  4. Transfer melted butter to a large mixing bowl. Add sugars and vanilla; beat about 3 minutes. Beat in egg.
  5. Add the dry ingredients, small amounts at a time, and stir with a spoon until combined.
  6. Use a 2 tablespoon measuring spoon to scoop out the dough and place them on a prepared baking sheet. Greased baking sheet if it is old or dark, non greased if it is newer or light colored metal. These will not spread at all, place them together.
  7. Bake 8 minutes at 350 and let cool. These will be firm, chewy, and similar to cookie dough.
