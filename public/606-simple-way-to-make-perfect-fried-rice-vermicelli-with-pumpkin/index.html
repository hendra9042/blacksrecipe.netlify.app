The ingredients needed to make 金瓜炒米粉 Fried Rice Vermicelli with Pumpkin:
  1. Prepare 金瓜 Pumpkin (1 slice)
  2. Take 米粉 Rice vermicelli (150g)
  3. Take 鸡蛋 Egg (2)
  4. Take 冬菇 Dried shiitake mushroom (2-4)
  5. Make ready 蒜米 Minced garlic (1 tablespoon)

Instructions to make 金瓜炒米粉 Fried Rice Vermicelli with Pumpkin:
  1. 浸泡冬菇(约6小时)，切片，冬菇水备用 Soak mushroom (~6hrs), cut into slices. Keep the soaked mushroom water
  2. 浸泡米粉(约1小时) Soak rice vermicelli for 1 hour
  3. 鸡蛋打散，煎了备用 Stir egg and fry
  4. 金瓜切丝 Cut pumpkin into thin slices
  5. 炒香蒜米，冬菇。加入金瓜，米粉 Fry minced garlic, mushroom. Add pumpkin, rice vermicelli
