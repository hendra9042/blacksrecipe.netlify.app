Instructions to make Egg sausage burrito:
  1. In a skillet fry the 2 patties till warmed through.
  2. In the same skillet with a little butter cook the eggs.
  3. Place tortilla on a baking sheet and add take a 2 pieces of cheese and lay it on the tortilla. Add the 2 patties and top with the eggs. Roll the tortilla up like a burrito and bake at 375 for 10 minutes. Top with salsa or hot sauce. Enjoy.
