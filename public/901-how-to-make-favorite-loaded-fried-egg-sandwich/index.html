The ingredients needed to make Loaded Fried Egg Sandwich:
  1. Take 1 tbsp Butter or margarine
  2. Get 1 Egg
  3. Make ready 3 slice Bacon cut in half
  4. Get 3 slice Turkey lunch meat -optional
  5. Prepare 2 slice Bread
  6. Get 1 Mayo
  7. Make ready 1 slice Cheese

Instructions to make Loaded Fried Egg Sandwich:
  1. Put butter in frying pan and let melt
  2. Fry egg in butter; make sure yoke is solid
  3. Place egg on a slice of bread and place cheese over so it melts on the egg
  4. Fry bacon and lunch meat then add to the sandwich
  5. Add mayo for taste
  6. Enjoy?
