The ingredients needed to make Fried Tofu with Eggs (Perkedel Tahu) *Vegetarian:
  1. Get 250 gr tofu
  2. Take 3 eggs
  3. Make ready 1 small red capsicum
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp pepper
  6. Prepare Red capsicum can be replaced with spring onion or celery
  7. Get Cooking oil to deep fry

Steps to make Fried Tofu with Eggs (Perkedel Tahu) *Vegetarian:
  1. Chop red capsicum, mash tofu well.
  2. In a moxing bowl, crack eggs, add tofu, capsicum, salt, and peper, mix well.
  3. Pre-heat frying pan with a lot of cooking oil.
  4. Take tofu mixture spoon by spoon and fry it into oil, cook until both side turn golden
  5. Take them out and serve them with any sauce you like.
