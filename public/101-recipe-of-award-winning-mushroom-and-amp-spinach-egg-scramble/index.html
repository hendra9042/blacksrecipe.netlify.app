The ingredients needed to make Mushroom & Spinach Egg Scramble:
  1. Get 7 Large Eggs
  2. Get 1/4 Whole Milk or Cream
  3. Get 1 Cup Sliced Mushrooms
  4. Take 2 Cup Fresh Spinach
  5. Prepare 3 Cloves Minced Garlic
  6. Take 2-3 Tbsp Olive Oil
  7. Make ready 1-2 Tbsp Butter
  8. Make ready Salt (To Taste)
  9. Take Black Pepper (To Taste)

Instructions to make Mushroom & Spinach Egg Scramble:
  1. Crack Eggs into a medium bowl. Add Milk, Salt, & Black Pepper. Whisk thoroughly and set aside.
  2. Heat Oil in a Large Skillet over Medium Heat. Add Butter. Allow to melt completely, then add Sliced Mushrooms. Lightly Salt and Pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally for several minutes, until Mushrooms are starting to brown.
  3. Add Garlic. Stir and cook for about 2 - 3 Minutes. Add Spinach Leaves. Stir for around a couple minutes, until the Spinach starts to wilt down.
  4. Add Eggs and allow to sit for few minutes, until the eggs begin to cook. Stir occasionally, until the eggs are nearly fully & evenly cooked. Turn off heat. Eggs will finish in the hot pan. Taste for seasoning.
  5. Serve and Enjoy!
