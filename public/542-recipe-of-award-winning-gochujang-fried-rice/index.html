The ingredients needed to make Gochujang fried rice:
  1. Prepare 2 cups leftover rice (refrigerated)
  2. Take 2 garlic (sliced)
  3. Get 3 Shallots (sliced)
  4. Take 2 Birdseye chili (sliced)
  5. Prepare 1 stalk spring onion (Chopped)
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp gochujang paste
  7. Get 1 tbsp sesame oil
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp cooking oil / butter
  9. Make ready 1 tsp fish sauce
  10. Prepare Salt
  11. Make ready Pepper
  12. Prepare Sugar
  13. Take Egg (fried)
  14. Prepare About 50ml of water (Optional)

Steps to make Gochujang fried rice:
  1. In a Pre-heated pan, add cooking oil and sesame oil, chili, garlic and Shallots. Cook till fragrant
  2. Once everything is fragrant, add gochujang paste, a little bit of water, salt, pepper and sugar. Cook it until it saucy and then add the rice, mix it until its all combined.
  3. Do a taste test, adjust the taste and then add some spring onion and some more sesame oil if you like.
