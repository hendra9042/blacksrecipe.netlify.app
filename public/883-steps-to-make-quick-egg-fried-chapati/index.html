Steps to make Egg fried chapati:
  1. Roll chapati n cut in to 1"thick strips
  2. Heat oil add chopped onions, chopped greenchilli n salt, make it till golden brown
  3. Break eggs beat well and add to the onion mixture and stir well as scramble egg
  4. Mean while take a pan heat n add rest of the oil prawns, turmeric, red chilli powder and ginger garlic paste n salt and fry till done
  5. Add pepper to the egg mixture and prawn fry wid the oil and masalas
  6. Now add the chapati which is cut into strips and mix well.
  7. Now add gravy to it and mix well until chapati observes the gravy. Turn off d heat n serve
