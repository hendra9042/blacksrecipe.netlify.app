The ingredients needed to make My Bagel + Avocado, Tomato, Poached Egg, Cream Cheese:
  1. Take 1/2 A Avocado sliced grilled
  2. Take 1 tbls cream cheese
  3. Take 1 Tomato
  4. Get 1 Slice Hallumi grilled
  5. Get 1 poached egg
  6. Prepare 1 Bagel sliced in two
  7. Make ready 1 little salt
  8. Prepare Some parsley for garnish
