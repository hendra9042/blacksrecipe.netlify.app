The ingredients needed to make Stir fried tomatoes and egg:
  1. Make ready 2 eggs
  2. Prepare 2 tomatoes
  3. Make ready Sausage or spam
  4. Get leaves Spinach
  5. Take 1 tbs garlic
  6. Make ready 1/2 tbs sugar
  7. Take 1/2 tbs soy sauce

Instructions to make Stir fried tomatoes and egg:
  1. Scramble two eggs in a bowl with some salt
  2. Slice up 2 tomatoes into wedges
  3. Slice sausages or spam into bite sized pieces
  4. In a frying pan, add cooking oil and scramble your eggs. Set aside in a bowl
  5. In the same pan, replace cooking oil and stir fry garlic until aromat. Add sausages and fry until cooked.
  6. Add tomatoes and cook until it is soft. Add 1/2 tbs sugar and 1/2tbs soy sauce. Let tomatoes cook until it is soft and juicy. You can add spinach leaves at this point.
  7. When tomatoes are at your preferred consistency, plop on your scrambled eggs.
  8. Serve with rice.
