The ingredients needed to make Classic egg tadka:
  1. Make ready 200 g urad daal (split toor daal)
  2. Make ready 100 gm channa daal (bengal gram daal)
  3. Prepare 1 onion (finely chopped)
  4. Take 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  5. Get 1/2 stem ginger (finely chopped)
  6. Get 4-5 garlic pod (finely chopped)
  7. Take 4-5 green chili (slit)
  8. Take 2 eggs (scrambled)
  9. Make ready Spices:
  10. Get 2-3 tbsp cumin powder
  11. Get 2-3 tbsp coriander powder
  12. Take 2-3 tbsp red chili powder
  13. Take 2 tbsp garam masala powder (can use whole cloves/cinnamon/cardamom)
  14. Prepare 4-5 tbsp kasauri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp methi (fenugreek)seeds
  16. Prepare 2tbsp butter
