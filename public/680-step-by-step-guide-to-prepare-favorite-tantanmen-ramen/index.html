The ingredients needed to make Tantanmen Ramen:
  1. Make ready Tare
  2. Make ready 9 tbsp Tsuyu (Japanese soup base sauce)
  3. Get 6 tbsp Tahini
  4. Get 3 tbsp chili oil
  5. Take Meat
  6. Get 500 g minced pork
  7. Take 6 tbsp chili oil, approximately
  8. Take 9 cloves garlic, chopped
  9. Get Half an inch ginger, chopped
  10. Get 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
  11. Make ready 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  12. Take 9 tbsp rice wine or any dry white wine
  13. Get Soup base
  14. Get 3 cups water
  15. Get 6 cups unsweetened soy, oats or cashew milk
  16. Prepare 2 chicken bouillon
  17. Get Noodles and toppings
  18. Make ready 3 portion dried or fresh ramen noodles, cooked per instruction

Instructions to make Tantanmen Ramen:
  1. To make the tare, mix all ingredients until well combined. Set aside.
  2. To cook the meat, heat the chili oil, and sauté the garlic and ginger for about 1 minute. Add the hoisin and oyster sauce and sauté for about 2 minutes. Add the meat and stir fry on high heat. Add a splash of wine and continue to saute the meat until there is no more moisture and the meat is cooked through. Set aside.
  3. To make the soup base, combine water, milk and chicken bouillon in a pot. Turn on heat and bring to a light simmer. Keep warm.
  4. To assemble, put about 2 tbsp of tare in the bottom of the bowl then then add the soup base. Add the ramen noodles and the toppings. Garnish with green spring onion onion and additional chili oil as desired.
