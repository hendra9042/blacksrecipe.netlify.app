Steps to make Motte saru poached egg sambar:
  1. Grind the ingredients for masala in to smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil in a wok add grounded masala paste and cook till raw smell goes off. Add required amount of water and salt and cook
  3. After sambar starts boiling break open the eggs into sambar do not stir. Cook for 7-10 minutes. Egg yolk will also be cooked hard.
  4. Serve hot with ragi mudde or Rice.
