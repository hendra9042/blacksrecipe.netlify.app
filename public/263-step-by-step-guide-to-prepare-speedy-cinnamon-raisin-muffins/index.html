The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Raisin Muffins:
  1. Make ready 4 cups instant pancake batter
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 1 cup raisins (or walnuts)
  4. Get 1 Tbsp softened butter
  5. Take 1 Tbsp cinnamon
  6. Get 1 cup granulated sugar
  7. Make ready 1 cup milk
  8. Take 2 Tbsp sesame seeds
  9. Make ready Glaze
  10. Take 2 cups powdered sugar
  11. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  12. Get 1 tsp melted butter
  13. Take 1/2 cup water

Fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients, until well incorporated. Spray your muffin tin with pan spray or line with paper liners.

Instructions to make Cinnamon Raisin Muffins:
  1. In a mixing bowl, add eggs and whisk. Add butter, raisins (or walnuts,) sugar, pancake batter, vanilla and cinnomon. Gradually add the milk and stir until batter is smooth but not to thick or thin.
  2. Preheat oven to 350° and put a thin layer of grease or vegetable oil spray in your Muffin pan.
  3. Poor your batter almost to the top of each Muffin cup, it will rise significantly during cooking.
  4. Bake at 350° for fifteen to twenty minutes or golden brown. Remove and let cool.
  5. Add Glaze and sprinkle with sesame seeds. Serve
