The ingredients needed to make Egg Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 4 eggs
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  3. Take 1 cup diced onion
  4. Make ready 4 cups cooked rice
  5. Prepare 1 tsp Soy sauce
  6. Take as per taste Salt
  7. Get 1/4 cup French beans,chopped
  8. Make ready few Garlic pods

Instructions to make Egg Fried Rice:
  1. Crack 4 eggs into a small bowl and beat them together.
  2. Take a pan. Add oil. Upon hearing add the onions and saute properly.
  3. When the onions turn golden brown, add garlic and saute. Add the beans and keep sauteeing.
  4. Add the eggs and mix properly.
  5. Add the rice. And add the salt and soy sauce accordingly.
  6. Sprinkle a bit of pepper powder and serve along with manchurian
