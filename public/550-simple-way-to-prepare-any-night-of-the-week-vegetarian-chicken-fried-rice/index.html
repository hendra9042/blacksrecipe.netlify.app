The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Chicken Fried Rice:
  1. Get 2 cups cooked rice
  2. Take Dash soy sauce
  3. Take 1 tbsp rice wine
  4. Prepare 1/4 onion, chopped
  5. Take 1 tbsp oil
  6. Make ready 1 cup seasoned plant-based chicken, grilled and cubed
  7. Prepare 1 carrot, diced
  8. Make ready 1 cup peas
  9. Get 2 eggs, scrambled
  10. Get Salt to taste
