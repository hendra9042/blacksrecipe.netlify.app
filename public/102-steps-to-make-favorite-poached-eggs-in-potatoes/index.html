The ingredients needed to make Poached eggs in potatoes:
  1. Prepare 4 potatoes
  2. Make ready Olive oil
  3. Prepare 15 ml tomato paste
  4. Prepare Water
  5. Prepare Fresh parsley, finely chopped
  6. Take 4 eggs

Instructions to make Poached eggs in potatoes:
  1. Cut your potatoes into blocks and wash your potatoes.
  2. Dry the potatoes.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a pan and fry the potatoes on a low heat for 20-40 minutes or until they are soft. Keep the lid on.
  4. Add the tomato paste, bit of water, finely chopped fresh parsley. Mix. You can also add bacon bits or other chopped vegetables.
  5. Make for dents in the mixture to add the eggs. Crack each egg into a dent.
  6. Close the the pan with the lid and fry for a few minutes until the egg is the consistency you like. I like my egg medium.
