Steps to make Clemson Fried Deviled Eggs:
  1. Boil eggs and cut in half.
  2. Put all yolks in bowl with all ingredients and mix until smooth.
  3. Take eggs whites place in flour, dredge in beaten egg, then cover in panko bread crumbs
  4. Fry for 2-3 minutes, let sit on paper towel to cool.
  5. Add smooth egg yolk mixture to eat egg. Serve and enjoy!
