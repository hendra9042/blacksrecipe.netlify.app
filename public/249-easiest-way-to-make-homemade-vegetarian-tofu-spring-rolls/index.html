The ingredients needed to make Vegetarian Tofu Spring Rolls:
  1. Take 1 pkg (350 g) Tofu (extra firm preferable)
  2. Make ready 1 medium carrots mined
  3. Get 6-7 green onions finely chopped
  4. Get 2 whole eggs
  5. Prepare 50 g rice vermicelli boiled and chopped
  6. Prepare 3 tablespoons corn starch
  7. Take 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  8. Take 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  9. Prepare 10-12 sheets Spring roll sheets
  10. Make ready Plum sauce
