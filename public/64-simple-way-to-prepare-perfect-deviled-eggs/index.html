The ingredients needed to make Deviled Eggs:
  1. Get 6 hard boiled eggs, peeled
  2. Make ready 3 tbs mayonnaise
  3. Take 1 tsp dijon mustard
  4. Take 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  5. Get 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
  6. Take Salt and black pepper to taste
  7. Prepare 1 cooked bacon, crumbled
  8. Take Ground paprika for garnish

Instructions to make Deviled Eggs:
  1. Remove the yolk to a small bowl with a spoon and place the egg whites on a plate.
  2. Mash the yolks with a fork and add the mayonnaise, mustard, garlic powder, vinegar, salt and pepper. Stir everything together until it's smooth.
  3. Add crumbled bacon. Mix it up.
  4. Use a spoon to add a portion of the deviled egg mixture back into the hole of each egg white. Sprinkle on paprika for garnish.
