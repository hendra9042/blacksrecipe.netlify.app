The ingredients needed to make Polenta, Spinach & Poached Egg:
  1. Get 4 cup Chicken Stock
  2. Prepare 2 cup Milk
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Butter
  4. Prepare 1 cup Polenta
  5. Take 500 grams Spinach
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp Butter
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  8. Prepare 2 Eggs
  9. Prepare 500 ml Water
  10. Get 1 tbsp Vinegar
  11. Get 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
  12. Get 1 Salt
  13. Make ready 1 Black Pepper
  14. Take 1 tbsp Butter

Steps to make Polenta, Spinach & Poached Egg:
  1. Bring stock and milk to the boil
  2. Add salt and pepper
  3. Add butter and stir until melted
  4. Add polenta
  5. While polenta is cooking melt butter in a pan and add olive oil
  6. Add spinach and allow to wilt
  7. Bring water and vinegar to boil
  8. Stir to form a current/swirl
  9. Drop eggs in one at a time and allow to set
  10. Add parmesan cheese and butter to cooked polenta and allow to melt by stirring well
  11. Dish polenta in a bowl, add spinach and egg
