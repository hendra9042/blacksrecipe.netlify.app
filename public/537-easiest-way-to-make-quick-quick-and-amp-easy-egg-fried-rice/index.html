The ingredients needed to make Quick & Easy Egg Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Prepare 2 tsp garlic powder
  3. Prepare 2 tsp onion powder
  4. Get 1/2 cup cooked rice
  5. Make ready 1 egg
  6. Prepare 1 tsp light soy sauce
  7. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  8. Make ready 2 tbsp chopped onion (dried)

Instructions to make Quick & Easy Egg Fried Rice:
  1. Heat oil and stir fry garlic and onion powders until fragrant.
  2. Add cooked rice, break up any clumps while cooking.
  3. Beat and pour egg mixed with soy sauce evenly over the rice and use high heat to fry until the rice is dry.
  4. Add salt for taste and sprinkle with dried onions.
