The ingredients needed to make Soft Fluffy Yeast Rolls:
  1. Make ready 2 1/4 teaspoons rapid rise dry yeast (1 package)
  2. Prepare 11/4 cup warm whole milk
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  4. Take 3 tablespoons unsalted melted butter, cooled to room temperature
  5. Prepare 1 large egg
  6. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  7. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  8. Get 4 cups all purpose flour
  9. Make ready melted salted butter for brushing
