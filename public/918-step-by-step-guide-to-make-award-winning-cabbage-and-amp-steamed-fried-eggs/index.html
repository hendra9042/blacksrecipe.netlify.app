Steps to make Cabbage & Steamed Fried Eggs:
  1. Mince the cabbage and vegetables. I used 20 g of carrots this time. Season the vegetables with salt and pepper.
  2. Add the vegetables to a deep heat-resistant container, cover with a lid and microwave for 30 seconds.
  3. Create a shallow well in the center and carefully place the egg on top. Use a toothpick to poke a hole in the egg yolk.
  4. Flavor the egg with salt and pepper if desired, close with a lid and microwave for a minute to a minute and a half, and it's complete.
  5. If you like your eggs stiff, then microwave the egg at the beginning with the vegetables.
