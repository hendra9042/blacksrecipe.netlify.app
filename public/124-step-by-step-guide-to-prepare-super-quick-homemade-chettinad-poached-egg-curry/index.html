Instructions to make Chettinad poached egg curry:
  1. Dry Roast the items under "To prepare Chettinad Masala" and grind them to fine powder.
  2. Heat 2tbsp oil, add in the garlic pods, ginger, onions, tomato and coconut. Saute till the onions and tomatoes are soft. Let it cool down and grind them to smooth paste by adding little water.
  3. Heat a tbsp of oil, add in the items under "To Tamper" and saute for a minute.
  4. Then Addin the grinded masala paste, 2tbsp of freshly prepared Chettinad masala. Mix well. 1. Further add in required water for the gravy and salt.
  5. Cover the pan and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. After 10 minutes, Break and drop the eggs into the boiling gravy. 1. Cover and cook for another 10 minutes. 1. 9.Chettinad Poached Egg Gravy is ready. Serve hot with Rice.
