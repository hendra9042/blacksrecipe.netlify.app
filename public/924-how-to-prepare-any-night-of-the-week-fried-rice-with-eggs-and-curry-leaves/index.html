The ingredients needed to make Fried Rice With Eggs And Curry Leaves:
  1. Get 2 tbsp oil
  2. Prepare 25 curry leaf
  3. Get 3 eggs
  4. Get 1 dash salt and pepper
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp dice shallot
  6. Make ready 1 tsp dice fine red chili pepper
  7. Take 2 cup cooked white rice
  8. Prepare 2 tbsp light soy sauce
