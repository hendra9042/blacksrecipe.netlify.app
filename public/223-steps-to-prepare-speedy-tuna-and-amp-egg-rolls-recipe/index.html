The ingredients needed to make Tuna & Egg Rolls Recipe:
  1. Take 200 Grams Tuna Flakes in Oil (remove water and oil)
  2. Make ready 2 Cups Cooked Rice
  3. Prepare 3 Pieces Eggs
  4. Take 1 Sheet Seaweed Nori
  5. Prepare 1 Tablespoon Roasted Sesame Seeds
  6. Make ready 1/2 Tablespoon Sesame Oil
  7. Make ready 4 Tablespoons Japanese Mayo
  8. Get Pinch salt
  9. Make ready Cooking Oil
  10. Take Chili powder (optional)

Steps to make Tuna & Egg Rolls Recipe:
  1. Removed the water and oil on Tuna Flakes.
  2. Add the Japanese Mayo and Set aside.
  3. Cutting Nori Sheets. Fold first in half then cut the top corner about 2 fingers size. Cut into string sizes and Set aside.
  4. In mixing bowl, combine cooked rice with Sesame Seeds and Sesame Oil then add a pinch of salt to taste.
  5. Cover Tuna mixture with Rice Mixture into rice ball repeat the process until done.
  6. Pre heat pan and add a little amount of cooking oil.
  7. Whisked 3 eggs and add salt to taste.
  8. Put the nori sheet string first and add egg mixture then put the rice ball and roll it until the egg fold the rice ball. Repeat the process.
  9. Serve. You can Visit my YouTube Channel "RJP Creatives" for video Tutorial.
