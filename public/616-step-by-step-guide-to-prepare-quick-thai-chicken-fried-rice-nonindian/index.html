The ingredients needed to make Thai Chicken Fried Rice #nonindian:
  1. Prepare 250 gms chicken thinly sliced
  2. Take 2 cups rice
  3. Make ready 6-8 cloves garlic
  4. Take 10-12 bird eye chilli
  5. Take 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  6. Take 1 tbsp sou sauce

  8. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Get 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  10. Make ready 1 big onion sliced
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup basil leaves
  13. Take Fried eggs

Steps to make Thai Chicken Fried Rice #nonindian:
  1. Boil rice with oil & salt. Strain & keep aside
  2. Marinate thinly sliced chicken in 2 tbsp oyster sauce, 1 tsp soy sauce & pepper & set aside.
  3. In a mortar pestle pound garlic, bird eye chilli & a pinch of salt into a rough paste & keep aside.
  4. In a bowl make stir fry sauce. Combine oyster sauce, soy sauce & sugar. Mix & keep aside.
  5. Deep fry eggs & keep aside
  6. Heat oil in a wok. Add onions & cook for a minute. - Add garlic & chill paste & cook for a minute. - Add marinated chicken & stir fry till chicken is well cooked. - Add sauce mix & mix well. - Add salt, pepper & rice & mix all well. - Lastly add basil leaves & give it a mix. - Remove from heat. - To serve divide rice among serving plates.
