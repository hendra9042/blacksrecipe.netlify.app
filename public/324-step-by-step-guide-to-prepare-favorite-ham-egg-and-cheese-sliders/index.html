Steps to make Ham egg and cheese sliders:
  1. Grease baking dish and preheat your oven to 350
  2. Cut Hawaiian rolls into individual rolls cut in half
  3. Lay bottoms of Hawaiian rolls in greased dish
  4. Fold 1 thinly sliced pieces of ham onto each roll
  5. Lay American cheese across tops of ham and roll bottoms. It’s ok to over lay over many sandwich bottoms as it will melt and sandwiches can be pulled apart
  6. Scramble your eggs and sprinkle over tops of cheese
  7. Put tops on your sandwiches
  8. Place in oven and bake until cheese melted and is 145 degrees. About 15 minuets
