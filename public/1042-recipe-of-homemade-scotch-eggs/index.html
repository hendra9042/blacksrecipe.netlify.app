Steps to make Scotch Eggs:
  1. Boil 4 eggs and unshell them
  2. Prepare the breadcrumbs and 2 eggs
  3. Add the black pepper and salt to the eggs and whisk
  4. Prepare the sausage
  5. Remove the outer covering of the sausage and mix the sausage to form a dough like mixture
  6. Cover the eggs one by one using sausage.
  7. Dip them in the eggs mixture
  8. Then dip in the breadcrumbs
  9. Dip all of them as you heat the cooking oil.
  10. Dip fry them till golden brown. Do not overcook.

  12. Cut into halves
