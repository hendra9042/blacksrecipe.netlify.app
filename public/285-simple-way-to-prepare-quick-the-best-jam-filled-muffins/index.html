The ingredients needed to make The Best Jam-filled Muffins:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cups flour
  2. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  3. Get 2 tsp baking powder
  4. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Get 1/4 cup melted coconut oil
  7. Take 1 cup 5 % greek vanilla yogurt (could also use 2%)
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup milk
  9. Prepare 1 egg
  10. Make ready 1/2 tsp vanilla
  11. Make ready Your favourite jam (approximately 1/2 cup)

In a large bowl, stir together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center. In a small bowl beat egg, milk and oil together. Pour all at once into the well in the flour mixture.

Instructions to make The Best Jam-filled Muffins:
  1. Blend dry ingredients together
  2. Melt coconut oil and add yogurt, milk, egg and vanilla.
  3. Add liquid ingredients to dry ingredients and mix for about 5 minutes.
  4. Grease a muffin pan and add half the batter into the wells.
  5. Place 1 tsp of jam in the centre of each well. My favourite are strawberry and raspberry.
  6. Top with remaining batter
  7. Bake at 375 for 20-25 minutes
  8. Dust with confectioner's sugar if you like
