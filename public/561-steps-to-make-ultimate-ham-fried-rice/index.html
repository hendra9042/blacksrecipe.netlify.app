The ingredients needed to make Ham Fried Rice (ハムチャハン):
  1. Take 2 cups leftover rice
  2. Prepare 2 slices ham, chopped into squares
  3. Prepare 1 egg, lightly beaten
  4. Get 2 stalks green onions, chopped, white and green parts divided
  5. Get to taste, salt and white pepper
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp bacon grease or vegetable oil

Instructions to make Ham Fried Rice (ハムチャハン):
  1. Heat 1 tbsp of bacon grease/oil in a pan or wok on medium heat. Lower the heat to medium low and fry the egg before it fully sets, about one minute. Set aside.
  2. Add one more tbsp oil/grease. Fry the ham and white parts of the green onion on medium heat for one minute.
  3. Add the rice, fluffing it up to break up whole portions. Season with salt and white pepper.
  4. Add in the egg, and mix it well. Add the soy sauce and continue fluffing the rice until it is well mixed.
