The ingredients needed to make Noom Friendly Whole Wheat Sweet Potato Muffins:
  1. Take 1 sweet potato
  2. Take 2 cups whole wheat flour
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  6. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  7. Get 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  8. Get 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup apple sauce
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup maple syrup
  11. Prepare 2 eggs lightly beaten
  12. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  13. Take 1 container (5-6oz) Greek vanilla yogurt

Instructions to make Noom Friendly Whole Wheat Sweet Potato Muffins:
  1. Preheat the oven to 400F. Wash the potato and prick it several times with a fork. Bake for 40 minutes on a baking sheet.
  2. The sweet potato should be soft; test with a fork. Peel and mash it.
  3. Set the oven to 350F.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine all the dry ingredients.
  5. Add the rest of the ingredients to the bowl and mix completely
  6. Grease 16 muffins cups or use paper liners. I tend to use liners. It is just easier.
  7. Spoon out the batter evenly amongst the cups.
  8. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes. Once completely cooled, you can easily decorate these muffins. Here I've used some marshmallow fondant to make little froggies.
