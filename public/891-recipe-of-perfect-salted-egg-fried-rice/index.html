The ingredients needed to make Salted egg fried rice:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup rice
  2. Make ready 1 salted egg
  3. Prepare 2 dried scallop
  4. Get 1 cup frozen vegetables
  5. Take 1 sesame oil
  6. Take 2 tbsp soy sauce
  7. Prepare 1 cup luncheon meat
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup onion, chopped
  9. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  10. Get 1 pinch pepper

Instructions to make Salted egg fried rice:
  1. Cook rice and refrigerate overnight to draw moisture from rice
  2. Soak dried scallops in water. After at least 3 hours, pull apart to strips. Set aside water used to soak dried scallops
  3. Cook frozen vegetables separately and set aside
  4. Add oil to medium hot pan
  5. Add onions and stir fry, for around 1 minute
  6. Add in luncheon meat, dried scallops and vegetables, stir fry for around 2 to 3 minutes
  7. Add in rice and toss
  8. Add salt, pepper and soy sauce
  9. Create well and add in salted eggs
  10. Cook eggs for around 2-3 minutes
  11. Add sesame oil to taste
