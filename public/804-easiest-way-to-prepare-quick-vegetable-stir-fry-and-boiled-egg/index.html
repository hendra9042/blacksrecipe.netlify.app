The ingredients needed to make Vegetable stir fry  and boiled egg:

  3. Get 4 Brussels sprouts (frozen)
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp butter
  5. Take 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp olive oil
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
  8. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  9. Make ready 2 boiled egg

Instructions to make Vegetable stir fry  and boiled egg:
  1. Take one pan and heat oil, butter in the pan.
  2. Add cauliflower, Brussels sprouts, carrot (can add any seasonal vegetables) and stir occasionally on medium heat.
  3. Let them to soft and light brown. Add black pepper and chaat masala and mix well.
  4. Remove them from pan. Keep on plate and add boiled egg. Serve with curd.
