The ingredients needed to make Ham And Egg With Cheese Fried Rice:
  1. Make ready 2 cup cooked leftover rice
  2. Prepare 2 eggs
  3. Take 1 tsp light soy sauce
  4. Prepare 1 cup thinly cured ham
  5. Get 1 1/2 tbsp butter
  6. Take SEASONING
  7. Make ready 1 dash spicy cajun spice seasoning
  8. Take 1 dash Italian mix herbs
  9. Prepare GARNISH OPTION CRISPY MINT
  10. Take 3 tbsp oil
  11. Prepare 6 mint leaves
  12. Get GARNISH OPTION CHEESY
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
